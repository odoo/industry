Odoo for Personal Trainer

This industry package includes everything you need to run your coaching business efficiently with Odoo.

Basics

  • Use the Appointment App to schedule and manage client sessions effortlessly.
  • Leverage the CRM to track leads and nurture client relationships.
  • Utilize the Sales App to create and manage your coaching services and packages.
  • Employ the Project App for long-term client program management and progress tracking.
  • Use the Invoicing App to bill clients and manage your finances.

Included Customizations
