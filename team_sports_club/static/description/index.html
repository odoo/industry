Odoo for Team Sport Clubs

This industry package includes all the essential features to manage your sports club end-to-end, from sponsorships and events to player management, fan engagement, and bookings.

Basics

  • Use the Teams App to organize rosters, assign players, and manage team compositions.
  • Manage sponsors and deals with the CRM App.
  • Sell tickets and event registrations through the Events App.
  • Operate the club bar and merchandise sales with the Point of Sale App.
  • Schedule field and court bookings with the Planning App.
  • Generate sponsorship offers and contribution requests with the Sales App.
  • Invoice and track payments with the Invoicing App.
  • Coordinate player recruitment and trials using the Project and Appointments Apps.
  • Showcase the club, teams, shop, and enrollments with your Website & eCommerce.

Customizations

  • Annual contribution requests directly from the Teams App with automatic Sales Order generation.
