Odoo for Team Sports Club

Score a winning goal in club management with this all-in-one Odoo industry package. It covers every base, from managing player rosters and organizing team compositions to securing lucrative sponsorships. The solution integrates fan engagement through events and ticketing with the operational side of running a club bar and merchandise store. By centralizing player recruitment, field bookings, and financial tracking, it empowers you to build a championship-level organization off the field.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Teams App: Organize your club's structure by managing rosters, assigning players to specific squads, and tracking team compositions.
  • CRM App: Professionalize your funding by tracking potential sponsors and managing sponsorship deals effectively.
  • Events App: Engage your fanbase by selling match tickets and managing registrations for tournaments or social events online.
  • Point of Sale App: Run your club's bar or merchandise shop efficiently, handling sales of drinks, snacks, and team jerseys.
  • Planning App: Optimize your facilities by scheduling field and court bookings to prevent conflicts between teams.
  • Sales App: Generate professional sponsorship offers and formal contribution requests to secure revenue.
  • Invoicing App: Keep the club's finances healthy by invoicing members and sponsors and tracking incoming payments.
  • Project App: Coordinate complex activities like player recruitment drives and trial days with clear tasks and timelines.
  • Website App: Build a digital home for your club to showcase team profiles, sell merchandise, and manage online enrollments.

Customizations

  • Contribution Management: Simplify revenue collection with annual contribution requests triggered directly from the Teams App, which automatically generates the necessary Sales Orders.

Pricing

This module involves multiple apps including Studio for various views, fields and relationships. It as well relies on lines of code through server actions and computed fields to bring extra capabilities, which leads to maintenance fees.
