Odoo for Community Care

Empower your care team with a unified platform designed for the unique needs of residential youth & social care services. By centralizing resident profiles, medical safety, and legal tracking, Odoo ensures that caregivers spend less time on paperwork and more time providing high-quality support. Gain peace of mind with a secure, structured environment for all sensitive resident data.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Contacts App: Maintain a secure database of residents, legal guardians, and external stakeholders with specialized categorization.
  • Documents App: Centralize legal orders, school reports, and individual care plans in a secure, searchable location.
  • Planning App: Coordinate staff shifts and 24/7 coverage to ensure resident safety and consistent care.
  • Calendar App: Schedule medical appointments, family visits, and therapy sessions with automated reminders.
  • Employees App: Centralize staff records, certifications, and hierarchies to ensure the right care team is always in place.
  • Timesheets App: Log hours spent on direct resident care, administrative tasks, or facility management for accurate reporting.
  • Time Off App: Manage staff absences and leave requests to maintain critical caregiver-to-resident safety ratios.
  • Knowledge App: Create collaborative logs for resident observations, track meeting outcomes, and use templates to efficiently draft formal reports for judges or social services.
  • Project App: Track long-term developmental goals or facility maintenance tasks through organized stages.

Customizations

  • Residents App: Access a dedicated workspace to manage your residents, from initial admission to successful transition.
  • Dedicated Resident Profiles: Transform standard contacts into comprehensive resident files with specialized views for Date of Birth, Age, and unique identifiers.
  • Relationship Mapping: Capture the vital network around a child with a dedicated contact list specifying the nature of relationships (e.g., Legal Guardian, Social Worker, Primary Physician).
  • Medical & Safety Dashboard: Instant visibility of critical data including blood type, allergies, health insurance details, and current medication protocols.
  • Placement & Legal Tracking: Manage the lifecycle of care with dedicated fields for admission dates, legal status (e.g., court-ordered, voluntary), projected exit dates, and special needs.
  • Identity & Inclusion: Respect the individuality of every resident by capturing nicknames, primary languages, and specific cultural or dietary requirements.
