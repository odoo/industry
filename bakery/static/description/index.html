Odoo for Bakery

This industry is ideal for bakeries specializing in bread, pastries, cakes, and custom orders, such as corporate breakfasts. It suits businesses serving walk-in customers, managing larger orders for cafes and restaurants, or delivering to multiple points of sale.

Basics

  • The Purchase App manages raw material orders and vendors.
  • The Point of Sale App manages in-store orders and payments.
  • The Sales App streamlines customer orders and quotations, such as managing company events or coffee shop orders.
  • The Website & eCommerce Apps enable online sales and offer Pick up at Store options.
