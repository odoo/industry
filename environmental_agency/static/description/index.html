Odoo for Environmental Agencies

This industry is ideal for environmental agencies focused on sustainability consulting, ESG assessments, and carbon footprint analysis. It is suited for organizations managing client relationships, large-scale projects, and delivering services to both public and private sectors.

Basics

  • The CRM App tracks client interactions and leads.
  • The Sales App simplifies quotations and contract management.
  • The Project & Documents Apps organizes projects and centralizes documents.
  • The Timesheet App tracks time and billable hours for impactful projects.
  • The Website & Appointment Appp showcases services and expertise and allows clients to book demos online.

Included customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
