Odoo for logistic company

This industry package includes all the essential tools for companies that offer third-party warehousing and logistics services, helping them manage client-owned inventory, warehouse operations, handling services, and transportation with full traceability and automated billing.

Basics

  • The inventory app enables precise stock management across multiple warehouses and clients, with full tracking of inbound, outbound, and internal movements.
  • The sales app generates quotations and sales orders for storage, transport, and value-added logistics services with custom pricing.
  • The CRM app tracks leads and opportunities, helping logistics companies follow up on prospects and manage customer relationships effectively.
  • The invoicing app automates billing based on storage duration, volume, and handling services, with support for deposits and custom pricing per client.
  • The website & portal app provides clients with real-time access to their stock levels, service requests, and downloadable invoices and delivery documents.
  • The subscriptions app handles recurring billing for storage services, with customizable plans and automated invoicing.
  • The barcode app streamlines warehouse operations by allowing fast and accurate scanning of pallets, locations, and stock movements.
  • The documents app organizes contracts, delivery notes, and transport documentation in a centralized and searchable digital space.

Customizations

  • Clients can see their current stock levels instantly on the portal.
  • Customize service fees for different location types.
  • Control the full consignment journey right from the product.
