Odoo for 3PL Logistic Company

This Odoo industry package is specifically engineered for third-party logistics providers. It streamlines the complex workflow of handling inbound and outbound shipments, ensuring full traceability from arrival to final delivery. The solution automates key processes like storage fee calculations and service billing, significantly reducing manual administrative work. With integrated tools for transportation and consignment tracking, logistics companies can offer their clients real-time visibility and superior service levels.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Inventory App: Enables precise stock management across multiple warehouses and clients, ensuring full tracking of inbound, outbound, and internal movements.
  • Sales App: Generates professional quotations and sales orders for storage, transport, and value-added logistics services.
  • CRM App: Tracks leads and opportunities efficiently, helping logistics companies follow up on prospects and manage long-term customer relationships.
  • Invoicing App: Automates billing calculations based on storage duration, volume, and handling services, with support for custom pricing per client.
  • Website & Portal: Provides clients with a self-service portal for real-time access to stock levels, service requests, and downloadable documents.
  • Subscriptions App: Manages recurring billing for long-term storage services with customizable plans.
  • Barcode App: Streamlines warehouse floor operations by enabling fast and accurate scanning of pallets, locations, and stock movements.
  • Documents App: Centralizes contracts, delivery notes, and transport documentation in a searchable digital space.

Customizations

  • Portal Visibility: Clients can see their current stock levels instantly on the portal.
  • Service Fees: Customize service fees for different location types to match your specific billing models.
  • Consignment Control: Control the full consignment journey right from the product view.

Pricing

This module involves multiple apps including Studio for various views, fields and relationships. It as well relies on lines of code through server actions and computed fields to bring extra capabilities, which leads to maintenance fees.
