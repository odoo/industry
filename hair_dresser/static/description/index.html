Odoo for Hair Salon

This industry package includes everything you need to run your hair salon efficiently with Odoo.

Basics 

  • Use the Appointments app for seamless scheduling of hair services. 
  • Create an attractive website to showcase your services and enable online booking.
  • Keep track of your product inventory with the Inventory app. 
  • Handle transactions and walk-ins using the Point of Sale system. 
  • Store and organize client information in the Contacts app. 
  • Streamline billing and financial management with the Invoicing app. 

Included customizations

  • Addition of a Break field in the appointment type. This field ensures a split in booking to free up the schedule of a staff member and optimize booking slots of various durations.
