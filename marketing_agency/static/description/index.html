Odoo for Marketing Agency

This Odoo industry package is the creative engine for your marketing agency. It is designed to handle the dynamic nature of client campaigns, from the initial pitch to the final performance report. The solution centralizes project management, allowing you to track tasks, deadlines, and creative assets in one place. By streamlining time tracking and billing, it empowers your team to focus on delivering high-impact work while ensuring that every billable hour is captured and invoiced accurately.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • CRM App: Track leads and opportunities in a visual pipeline, ensuring you never miss a potential client pitch or follow-up.
  • Project App: Plan and execute complex marketing campaigns with task lists, Kanban boards, and milestones to keep your team on deadline.
  • Timesheets App: Track time spent on client projects effortlessly, ensuring accurate billing for hourly work and better resource planning.
  • Subscriptions App: Manage recurring revenue streams for retainer clients, automating monthly invoicing and renewal management.
  • Social Marketing App: Manage social media campaigns for multiple clients from one central dashboard, scheduling posts and tracking engagement.
  • Planning App: Schedule your creative team's workload visually to prevent burnout and ensure the right talent is assigned to the right project.
  • Website App: Showcase your agency's portfolio and case studies with a professional, easy-to-update website that attracts new business.
