Odoo for Marketing Agencies

This industry package includes everything you need to run your marketing agency efficiently with Odoo.

Basics

  • Use the CRM App to track leads and opportunities, manage your sales pipeline, and never miss a potential client.
  • Use the Sales App to create professional proposals and manage your service offerings.
  • Leverage the Subscriptions App to set up recurring revenue streams for retainer clients.
  • Use the Project App to plan and track all your client projects with task management and milestone.
  • Track time spent on client work with the Timesheets App for accurate billing and resource planning.
  • Use the Planning App to schedule your team's workload and manage resource allocation.
  • Showcase your agency's portfolio and services with the Website App to attract new clients.
  • Manage social media campaigns for your clients from one dashboard with the Social Marketing App.
  • Use the Invoicing App to bill clients professionally and track payments.
  • Organize client workshops, training sessions, and marketing events with the Events App.

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
