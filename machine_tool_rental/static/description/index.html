Odoo for Industrial Equipment

This industry package includes everything you need to run a business that rents out machines or equipment like aerial lifts, excavators, compressors, and construction tools.

Basics

  • The CRM App capture customer leads and help turn them into contracts with automated workflows.
  • The Rental App will centralize rental contracts, billing cycles, availability, and reservations.
  • The Invoicing App supports recurring billing, deposits, late fees, and equipment damage charges.
  • The Inventory App will manage the physical stock of machines, keep track of detail serial numbers, track usage and returns.
  • The Field Service App manages delivery, installation, on-site intervention schedules as well as regular servicing and reduces downtime by planning maintenance cycles.

Customisations

  • Run-Time Meter on serials with recording on rental returns and monitoring from Inventory App.
  • Maintenance Rules automate planning of machine and tool maintenance tasks.
  • Consolidated down-payment invoicing based on consumption.
  • Rental return quality control with automated task planning on failure.
