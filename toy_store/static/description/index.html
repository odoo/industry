Odoo for Toy Store

Manage your toy kingdom efficiently with this specialized Odoo setup. It handles the complexity of managing thousands of product variants—from action figures to board games—ensuring accurate stock levels across all channels. The integrated POS system provides a fast, child-friendly checkout experience, while the eCommerce app lets you sell 24/7. This package simplifies the business side of play, allowing you to focus on curating the best selection for your customers.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Point of Sale App: Speed up transactions during the holiday rush with an intuitive interface that handles barcodes and loyalty cards effortlessly.
  • Inventory App: Manage a massive catalog of references with ease, tracking variants like color, size, and character models to prevent discrepancies.
  • eCommerce App: Create a magical online shopping experience, allowing customers to browse by age, category, or brand from home.
  • Purchase App: Automate reordering based on stock levels to ensure you never run out of best-selling toys and games.
  • CRM App: Build a loyal base of parents and collectors by tracking purchase history and sending personalized birthday offers.
  • Website App: Design a colorful and engaging website that showcases your latest arrivals and store events.
