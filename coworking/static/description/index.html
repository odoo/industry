Odoo for Coworking Spaces

This suite of applications is designed to meet the needs of coworking spaces; whether you manage a boutique shared office, a multi-location coworking hub, or a community-driven workspace. With Odoo, you can centralize your operations in one place: from selling memberships and booking meeting rooms to engaging your community and organizing staff schedules.

Basics

  • CRM & Membership: Track and manage coworking opportunities, follow-up, and convert them into active members.
  • Subscription: Automate recurring memberships such as Business Address or Unlimited Monthly Access.
  • Sales: Generate and manage quotations for memberships, room bookings, and services.
  • Appointment: Allow prospects to book a free tour, shared desk, or meeting room online.
  • Website: Promote coworking offers, showcase events, and provide member-exclusive tools and support forms.
  • Events: Organize networking sessions, workshops, and community-building activities.
  • Email Marketing: Share news, updates, and promotions with your members.
  • Helpdesk: Handle member requests and issues efficiently.
  • Planning: Schedule reception desk coverage and other staff shifts.

Customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
