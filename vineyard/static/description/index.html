Odoo for Vineyard

Designed for modern winemakers, this Odoo industry solution provides a comprehensive toolkit for managing the grape-to-bottle lifecycle. It allows you to monitor crop health, manage harvest yields, and oversee complex manufacturing processes like pressing and blending. The system ensures full traceability and simplifies excise management, reducing administrative burdens. With integrated features for direct sales and winery tours, it helps you build a strong brand and connect directly with wine lovers.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • CRM App: Cultivate relationships with distributors and private clients, tracking leads to close more deals effectively.
  • Manufacturing App: Manage the art of winemaking by overseeing pressing, fermentation, and blending processes with precision.
  • Quality App: Ensure every bottle meets your standards by tracking quality control points throughout production.
  • PLM App: Innovate your wine portfolio by iterating on blend recipes and managing product lifecycle changes.
  • Field Service App: Coordinate vineyard operations, assigning tasks to teams for pruning, spraying, and harvesting.
  • Point of Sale App: Manage direct sales at the cellar door with an easy-to-use interface for visitors and tourists.
  • Appointments App: Organize and book winery tours and tastings, enhancing the visitor experience.
  • Website App: Sell your wines globally with a fully integrated eCommerce platform that manages inventory in real-time.

Customizations

  • Excise Management: Simplify compliance with specialized tools for Excise Management, ensuring accurate tax reporting.
  • Harvest Management: Gain insights into your crop with detailed harvest receptions and yield reporting.
  • Scrap Monitoring: Optimize production efficiency with a dedicated dashboard for scrap ratio monitoring to reduce waste.

Pricing

This module involves multiple apps including Studio for various views, fields and relationships. It as well relies on lines of code through server actions and computed fields to bring extra capabilities, which leads to maintenance fees.
