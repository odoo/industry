Odoo for Veterinarians

This Odoo industry package is the ultimate assistant for modern veterinary clinics, designed to manage patient care and clinic operations in one unified system. It streamlines the entire consultation process, from online appointment booking to maintaining detailed medical history charts for every pet. The solution simplifies billing with integrated payment terminals and ensures you never miss a vaccination reminder. By reducing administrative tasks, it empowers veterinarians to focus on what matters most: the health and happiness of their animal patients.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Appointments App: Streamline the booking process for pet owners, allowing them to schedule check-ups and surgeries online 24/7.
  • Point of Sale App: Facilitate a smooth checkout experience by linking consultations and product sales directly to your payment terminal.
  • Website App: Build a professional online presence to market your clinic, share health tips, and attract new pet owners.
  • Marketing App: Engage your community with targeted email campaigns for vaccination reminders and seasonal health check-ups.
  • Invoicing App: Simplify your finances by automatically generating invoices from completed consultations and product sales.
  • Contacts App: Maintain a clean directory of pet owners, linked directly to their animal's medical profiles for easy access.

Customizations

  • Pet Management: Utilizing a custom application, manage pets and veterinarian consultations effectively, linking every visit to the specific animal.
  • Medical Tracking: Track critical health data with new models designed for vaccines, pet species, diseases, and medical history.
  • Enhanced Booking: Experience an enhanced appointment screen tailored to support pet owners and their specific animals during booking and check-in.
  • Quick Access: Navigate effortlessly between records with a smart button that links pets to their owner's contact profile instantly.

Pricing

This module involves multiple apps including Studio for various views, fields and relationships. It as well relies on lines of code through server actions and computed fields to bring extra capabilities, which leads to maintenance fees.
