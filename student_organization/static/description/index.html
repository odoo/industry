Odoo for Student Organization

This Odoo industry package is the campus essential for managing dynamic student bodies. It centralizes member management, allowing you to track subscriptions, renewals, and alumni networks effortlessly. The solution integrates event planning tools to organize parties, workshops, and meetups, selling tickets directly through a dedicated website. By streamlining financial tracking and communication, it empowers student leaders to focus on building community rather than drowning in spreadsheets.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Members App: Manage your membership base efficiently, tracking active status, dues payments, and renewal dates.
  • Events App: Create and promote student events, handling registrations and ticket sales online for everything from galas to workshops.
  • Website App: Build a portal for your organization to share news, host a calendar, and allow members to sign up for activities.
  • Accounting App: Keep track of the organization's budget, managing expenses and income from fees and fundraising.
  • Email Marketing App: Engage the student body with newsletters about upcoming events, elections, and important announcements.
  • eCommerce App: Sell branded merchandise, event kits, or study materials directly to your members online.
