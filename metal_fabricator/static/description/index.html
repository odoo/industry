Odoo for Metal Fabricator

The custom building products manufacturing industry specializes in creating made-to-order components for construction projects. While our data currently highlights the window sector, this concept applies to all bespoke building elements, from specialized doors and facade systems to custom cabinetry and structural components.

Basics

  • The CRM & Sales app's Manages all product and service charges for your clients.
  • The Manufacturing App handles all product creations.
  • The Purchase App ensures accurate inventory replenishment and tracks subcontracting services.
  • The Project App is ideal for managing complex projects that involve multiple production stages.
  • The Invoice App helps you keep track of all your invoices.
  • The Website App acts as a virtual showroom and a powerful tool for generating new leads.

    • This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
