Odoo for Metal Fabricator

This Odoo industry package is forged for the specific needs of custom building product manufacturers. It specializes in handling make-to-order components, from intricate facade systems to heavy structural elements. The solution seamlessly integrates your design and sales teams with the shop floor, ensuring that every custom specification is accurately translated into production orders. By managing complex projects and subcontracting in one system, it ensures your fabrication timelines are met with precision.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Manufacturing App: Handle the creation of all your products, managing bills of materials (BoMs) and work centers for everything from cutting to welding.
  • Project App: Manage complex fabrication projects that involve multiple production stages and installation phases efficiently.
  • Purchase App: Ensure accurate inventory replenishment for raw materials and track subcontracting services for specialized treatments like galvanizing.
  • CRM & Sales App: Manage all product and service charges for your clients, creating detailed quotations for bespoke items.
  • Website App: Act as a virtual showroom to display your past projects and specialized capabilities, serving as a powerful tool for generating new leads.
  • Inventory App: Keep strict control over your raw metals, hardware, and finished components across your warehouse and production floor.
  • Invoicing App: Keep track of all your invoices, handling progress billing for long-term projects or standard invoicing for direct sales.
