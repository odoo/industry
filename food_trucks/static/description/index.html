Odoo for Food Trucks

This industry package includes all your essential apps to manage your food truck business : CRM, Invoicing, Field Service & Point-of-Sale.

Basics

  • Use the CRM App to manage your opportunities
  • Send quotations to prospective customers through the Sales app.
  • Manage your presence at events using the Field Service app.
  • Invoice your customers using the Invoicing app.

Included customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
