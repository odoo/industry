Odoo for Clothing Store

This Odoo industry package is a perfect fit for fashion retailers, offering a curated solution to manage stylish dresses, accessories, and seasonal collections. It simplifies the complexity of apparel retail by handling product variants—like size, color, and fabric—with ease. The solution integrates your physical boutique with an elegant online store, ensuring that your latest trends are visible to customers everywhere. By streamlining inventory and sales, it empowers you to focus on showcasing your unique style and attracting new shoppers.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Point of Sale App: Speed up checkout with an intuitive interface that handles multiple variants, returns, and customer loyalty programs seamlessly.
  • Inventory App: Manage complex product matrices (sizes, colors, fits) and use barcode/RFID scanning for rapid stock counts and transfers.
  • eCommerce App: Create a stunning online storefront to sell your fashion collections globally, offering features like "shop by look" or dynamic filters.
  • Sales App: Handle wholesale orders or B2B contracts with ease, using variant grids to quickly enter large orders for different sizes.
  • Purchase App: Automate replenishment based on seasonal demand, ensuring you have the right mix of sizes and styles in stock.
  • Website App: Design a visually appealing brand site that showcases your latest arrivals, lookbooks, and store locations.
  • Email Marketing App: Engage your customers with personalized newsletters about new collections, sales, and exclusive style advice.
