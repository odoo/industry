Odoo for Clothing Stores

This industry package includes all the essentials for managing your clothing store, from product management to in-store checkout, and website.

Basics

  • Point of Sale App: Speed up checkout with an intuitive interface that handles product variants, returns, and customer loyalty programs seamlessly.
  • eCommerce & Website App: Create a stunning online storefront to sell your fashion collections globally, offering features like "shop by look" or dynamic filters.
  • Purchase App: Automate replenishment based on seasonal demand, ensuring you have the right mix of sizes and styles in stock.
  • Inventory App: Manage complex product matrices (sizes, colors, fits) and use barcode/RFID scanning for rapid stock counts and transfers.
  • Website App: Design a visually appealing brand site that showcases your latest arrivals, lookbooks, and store locations.
  • Email Marketing App: Engage your customers with personalized newsletters about new collections, sales, and exclusive style advice.
