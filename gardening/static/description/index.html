Odoo for Gardeners

This industry package includes everything you need to run your gardening company efficiently with Odoo.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Use the CRM and the Sales Apps to manage your customer relations, your opportunities and create stunning quotations.
  • Manage your sites using the Project Apps, and smaller tasks (mowing...) using the Field Service App.
  • Keep track of your stock with the Inventory App. Replenish using the Purchase App.
  • Follow your planning and keep track of worked time in the Planning & Timesheets Apps.
  • Invoice at the right time with the Invoice App.

Included customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
