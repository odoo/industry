Odoo for Arts & Crafts Store

Tailored specifically for the world of creativity, this Odoo industry package offers a complete solution for arts and crafts stores. It effectively manages a diverse range of inventory with complex variants, ensuring that you can always meet customer demand for specific colors or materials. The system integrates powerful tools for POS and eCommerce, making it easier to run your store efficiently both online and offline. It is the essential tool for combining artistic passion with professional business management.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Point of Sale App: Delight customers with a fast checkout experience, easily handling thousands of small items like beads, ribbons, and brushes.
  • Inventory App: Master the complexity of product variants, keeping track of every color, size, and material type to prevent stockouts of best-sellers.
  • eCommerce App: Expand your creative reach by selling supplies online, offering a convenient shopping experience for hobbyists everywhere.
  • Purchase App: Automate the reordering of essential crafting materials, ensuring your shelves are always stocked with the tools your customers need.
  • CRM App: Build a loyal community of makers by tracking customer interests and engaging them with personalized updates on new arrivals.
  • Website App: Create an inspiring online space that not only sells products but showcases creative projects and ideas to drive engagement.

Customizations

  • Consignment Purchases: Effortlessly manage inventory held on behalf of artists by enabling consignment tracking on RFQs, which automatically records ownership upon reception to simplify returns of unsold goods.

Pricing

This module involves multiple apps including Studio for various views, fields and relationships. It as well relies on lines of code through server actions and computed fields to bring extra capabilities, which leads to maintenance fees.
