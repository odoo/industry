Odoo for DIY Workshops

This industry package is designed for DIY studios; whether you run sewing classes, woodworking sessions, or host pottery and painting workshops. Odoo helps you manage it all in one place: workshops management, online bookings, material purchases, membership, and private workshop requests.

Basics

  • The Events App organizes creative workshops like sewing, painting, and woodworking to engage your community.
  • The Website App promotes your studio, explains your creative offerings, and enables online class bookings.
  • The Sales App simplifies private workshop requests, material sales, and manages memberships.
  • The Purchase App manages your supplies by tracking and ordering fabrics, clay, tools, paints, and more.
  • The Invoicing App automates payments and keeps your finances organized.

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
