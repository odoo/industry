Odoo for Fast Food Restaurant

This industry package includes everything you need to run your Fast Food Restaurant efficiently with Odoo.

Basics

  • Use the Point of Sale at the desk for your sales. You can also download the Odoo Mobile App on any phone to take orders. And create Kiosks or QR Codes to ensure self-ordering.
  • The Kitchen Displays will ensure a great follow-up of every order in your bar and kitchen.
  • Use the Planning App to schedule and share your shifts with your employees.
  • Use the Purchase App to reorder your products.
  • Use the Inventory App to manage your stock and receive products.

Included customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
