Odoo for Automobile Spare Parts

This Odoo industry package is a tailored solution for automobile part trading companies, designed to streamline B2B and B2C sales. It effortlessly manages complex inventories of spare parts, engine components, and accessories across various car brands. The system integrates essential operations like Point of Sale, Sales, and Purchasing into a single efficient workflow. With built-in eCommerce capabilities, you can easily expand your business online, offering a seamless experience to mechanics and car owners alike.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Point of Sale App: Speed up counter sales for spare parts and accessories with a user-friendly interface designed for high-volume retail.
  • Inventory App: Manage a vast catalog of automotive parts with precision, tracking stock levels across brands, models, and compatibility.
  • Sales App: Handle B2B orders for garages and fleet partners efficiently, creating professional quotes and managing contract pricing.
  • Purchase App: Automate the replenishment of fast-moving items like filters and oil, ensuring your shelves are always stocked.
  • eCommerce App: Launch a robust online store to sell parts directly to consumers, with search filters for vehicle compatibility.
  • Planning App: Organize team shifts and resource allocation to ensure your shop floor or warehouse is always adequately staffed.

Customizations

  • OEM and EAN codes: Capture OEM and EAN code on products for full traceability.

Pricing

This module involves multiple apps including Studio for various views, fields and relationships. It as well relies on lines of code through server actions and computed fields to bring extra capabilities, which leads to maintenance fees.
