Odoo for Dropshipping

This industry package includes all the essentials to establish a new dropshipping business.

Basics

  • Use the Website app to create beautiful landing pages
  • Use the Ecommerce app for a quick and easy shopping experience for your customers
  • Customer orders create Dropshipping purchase orders that get sent straight to your suppliers
  • Manage your marketing needs through the Email marketing, Social marketing and Marketing Automation apps

Included customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
