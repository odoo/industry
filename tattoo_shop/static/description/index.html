Odoo for Tattoo Shops

This industry package includes all the essential features to manage your tattoo shop, from customer relationship management to consent form signatures and appointments.

Basics

  • Use the CRM App to manage your leads and track ongoing tattoo projects.
  • Handle consent forms and important documents effortlessly with the Sign App.
  • Showcase your work, attract new clients through the Website App.
  • Let your customers book and manage appointments through the Appointments App.
  • Share your latest creations and engage with your audience using the Social Marketing App.
  • Restock supplies with ease using the Purchase App.
  • Manage your opportunities through Activities.

Customisations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
