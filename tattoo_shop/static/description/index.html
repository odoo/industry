Odoo for Tattoo Shop

This Odoo industry package inks efficiency into every aspect of your tattoo shop management. It seamlessly connects the artistic side of your business with the operational essentials, handling everything from customer appointments to inventory replenishment. The solution simplifies the critical process of managing digital consent forms, ensuring they are signed and stored securely before the needle touches skin. By centralizing artist schedules and client communications, it empowers you to focus on creating art while the software handles the shop floor.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • CRM App: Manage your leads effectively, tracking ongoing tattoo projects and consultations to ensure no client request is lost.
  • Sign App: Handle critical compliance by managing consent forms and important waivers effortlessly with secure digital signatures.
  • Website App: Build a stunning online portfolio to showcase your work and attract new clients with a professional digital presence.
  • Appointments App: Streamline your schedule by letting customers book and manage their own sessions online based on artist availability.
  • Social Marketing App: Amplify your reach by sharing your latest creations across social platforms and engaging with your audience centrally.
  • Purchase App: Automate the restocking of essential supplies like ink, gloves, and needles to ensure you never run out during a session.
