Odoo for Bike Shop

This industry package includes all the essential tools a bike shop needs to run daily operations in one place. It covers everything from selling bikes; both online through an eCommerce website and in-store with a point of sale system, to managing repairs, maintenance services, and inventory.

Basics

  • The Point of Sale (PoS) manages bike and accessory sales directly in the store.
  • The Sales App create and manage quotations and sales orders.
  • The eCommerce App sell bikes and accessories online with a customizable web store.
  • The Field Service and Project App organizes and tracks repair tasks, deadlines, and parts used in the workshop.
  • The Inventory App tracks bike and parts stock, including serial numbers and stock movement.
  • The Purchase App manages vendor orders and restocks bikes and parts.
  • The Website App manages your online presence and allow customers to book appointments or test rides.
  • The Helpdesk App manages warranty claims, after-sales support, and repair requests.
