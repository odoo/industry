Odoo for Cosmetics Store

This industry package is designed for beauty businesses of all sizes; whether you're running a retail boutique, managing a skincare brand, or selling makeup online. Odoo helps you manage everything in one place: sales, inventory, eCommerce, purchases, and customer loyalty.

Basics

  • The Point of Sale App enables in-store sales of skincare, makeup, and beauty tools using a barcode or touch-screen interface.
  • The Website & eCommerce App supports online sales of cosmetic products through a brand-focused website.
  • The Sales App manages wholesale and custom client orders, including the creation of professional quotations.
  • The Purchase App handles the restocking of ingredients, packaging, and finished goods from preferred suppliers.
  • The Inventory App tracks cosmetic stock levels in real time and automates reordering processes.
  • The Invoicing App automates customer billing and manages payments, refunds, and credit notes.

Customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
