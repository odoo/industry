Odoo for Property Owner Associations

This Odoo industry package is the ultimate tool for professional property managers and syndics. It is designed to handle the complex legal and financial structure of managing multiple condominiums simultaneously. The solution uses a multi-company architecture to ensure that every buildingâ€™s funds and accounting are kept strictly separate. By automating fund calls and expense distribution based on ownership keys, it empowers you to manage more buildings with less administrative effort.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Multi-Company: Configure each condominium as a separate entity to ensure strict segregation of funds and accounting records, while managing them from a single interface.
  • CRM App: Track opportunities to acquire new building management contracts and grow your portfolio.
  • Contacts App: Maintain a centralized directory of unit owners, tenants, and maintenance suppliers.
  • Sales App: Bill the condominium associations for your management fees and manage fund raising campaigns for major works.
  • Subscription App: Automate the collection of recurring common charges and monthly fees from owners.
  • Accounting App: Handle the full accounting lifecycle for each condominium, ensuring balanced books and transparent financial reporting.
  • Documents App: Archive critical documents like bylaws, insurance policies, and contracts to prevent data loss.
  • Project App: Manage maintenance tasks, renovation projects, and follow-ups on resident requests.
  • Timesheets App: Track the time your employees spend working on specific buildings to analyze profitability or justify management fees.
  • Employees App: Assign property managers to specific buildings and manage your internal team's workload.
  • Calendar App: Schedule board meetings, maintenance visits, and annual general assemblies.

Customizations

  • Condominium Management App: A dedicated application to configure buildings and units, defining the structure of the property.
  • Split Sale Orders: Automatically split fund requests among owners according to specific distribution keys (e.g., thousandths) using analytic accounts.
  • Split Invoices: Automatically split supplier invoices and expenses among owners based on distribution keys or meter readings via analytic accounts.
  • Owner History: Capture ownership history at the unit level, enabling accurate cost splitting via analytic accounts even when ownership changes during the fiscal year.
  • General Meetings: Transform Calendar events into formal General Meetings, featuring tools for presence checks, agenda management, voting mechanisms, and automatic report generation.
