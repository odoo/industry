Odoo for Public Institution

This Odoo industry package is designed to help public institutions deliver efficient, transparent, and citizen-centric services. It centralizes administrative processes, streamlines communication with residents, and digitizes public services such as appointments, document signing, facility rentals, and citizen support. By integrating multiple Odoo applications into a single solution, public institutions can improve operational efficiency while providing a modern digital experience for citizens.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Helpdesk App: Tracks citizen inquiries, pothole reports, and complaints from initial submission through to final administrative resolution.
  • Appointments App: Provides a 24/7 self-service portal for citizens to book meetings with officials or schedule document renewals.
  • Website App: Acts as the digital town square where residents find information, public services, and official updates.
  • Sign App: Facilitates legally binding digital signatures for permits, petitions, and internal approvals, eliminating the need for physical paperwork.
  • Rental App: Manages the availability, reservations, and payments for community halls, sports fields, and public equipment.
  • Events App: Organizes, promotes, and tracks attendance for local fairs, town hall meetings, and community workshops.
  • Survey App: Collects community feedback on public initiatives, urban planning, and local service satisfaction.
  • Project App: Structures council agendas and tracks administrative tasks to ensure accountability across all municipal departments.
  • Blog App: Publishes official news, emergency alerts, and community stories to keep citizens informed.
