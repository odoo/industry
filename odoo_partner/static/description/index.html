Odoo for Odoo Partner

This Odoo industry package is the operating system for successful Odoo partnerships. It is designed to manage the unique lifecycle of selling and implementing Odoo solutions, from the first demo to the final go-live. The solution centralizes your sales pipeline, success packs, and customer support into one cohesive platform. By streamlining implementation projects and tracking billable hours efficiently, it empowers you to scale your partnership while ensuring high customer satisfaction and retention.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • CRM App: Manage your leads and opportunities in a dedicated pipeline, tracking stages from "New Lead" to "Won" efficiently.
  • Sales App: Create professional proposals using an advanced Quote Calculator and PDF Quote Builder to impress prospective clients.
  • Project App: Manage Success Packs and large implementations with task management, milestones, and shared customer views.
  • Helpdesk App: Handle client support requests and tickets systematically, ensuring timely resolution and high service levels.
  • Timesheets App: Track billable hours spent on client projects to ensure accurate invoicing and profitability analysis.
  • Planning App: Schedule your consultants' and developers' workload to optimize resource allocation and prevent bottlenecks.
  • Documents App: Store and organize crucial client documents, contracts, and technical specifications in a secure, central repository.
  • Sign App: Send contracts and engagement letters for fast, legally binding digital signatures.
