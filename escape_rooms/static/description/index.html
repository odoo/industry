Odoo for Escape Rooms

This industry package includes all the things you need for your Escape Room business, from your website, to your booking system, and your on-site point of sale.

Basics

  • Website 🌐 : Show rooms, stories, prices; capture reservations in real time, sell gift cards
  • Appointments 📅 : Let guests pick room, date, time from your live calendar, turns choices into confirmed slots, locks room capacity.
  • Point of Sale 🛒 : Sell walk-ins, snacks, drinks, souvenirs with barcode speed.
  • CRM 🎯 : Track inquiries, group events, corporate deals, repeat-player campaigns.

Customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
