Odoo for Bar & Pub

This module offers a vibrant selection of cocktails, soft drinks, and other essential items tailored for the bar and pub industry. It is an invaluable resource for elevating establishments, enhancing customer experiences, and exploring new trends in mixology and hospitality.

Basics

  • Use the Point of Sale for your sales at the desk. You can also download the Odoo Mobile App on any phone to take orders.
  • The Kitchen Display will ensure a great follow up of every order in your bar and kitchen.
  • Use the Project App to never miss a reordering or a cleaning task.
  • Use the Planning App to schedule your shifts and share it with your employees.
  • Use the Purchase App for your sales at the desk. You can also download the Odoo Mobile App on any phone to take orders.
  • Use the Inventory App to manage your stock and receive products.
  • Use the Attendances App to record employee's time spent working.

Included customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
