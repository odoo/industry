Odoo for Camp Site

This industry package is designed to run your Camp Site operations. It provides a first set products, resources and tools to plan stays even online, welcome guests and manage city tax.

Basics

  • Online booking from eCommerce / Website App for your customers.
  • Leverage Rental services and Planning roles binding to prevent double booking.

Customizations

  • Manage plan, check in and check out your bookings from Campsite App.
  • Interactions between Rental orders and Planning shifts to manage booking from a Gantt view.
  • City tax computed based on bookings in Rental orders.
  • Guest identity recorded in Contacts App and related to Rental orders.
