Odoo for Non-profit organization

This industry package is built for NGOs of all sizes; whether you're supporting local communities, protecting the environment, or advocating for human rights. Odoo helps you manage everything in one place: donations, petitions, volunteers, events, memberships, and communication.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Community to engage supporters, track donations, and drive collective action through petitions.
  • Petition tracks signatures, signatories, and progress for each petition.
  • Project structures campaigns, assigns tasks, and tracks progress.
  • Donors, members, signatories, and partners are organized in Contacts.
  • Follow-ups and communication logs are handled efficiently with CRM.
  • Create events to raise awareness, coordinate volunteer activities, or host annual general assemblies.
  • Promote events, create donation and petition forms, and sell merchandise online using Website & eCommerce.
  • Recurring memberships and automated renewals are managed by Subscription.
  • Volunteer shifts and roles are scheduled and tracked through Planning.
  • Thank-you notes for donors, petition follow-ups, and newsletters are sent using Email Marketing.

Customizations

  • Community App
