Odoo for Bowling Alley

This industry package includes all the essential apps and configurations for small to medium bowling alley businesses to manage bookings, food orders, and a beautiful website in just a few clicks. Everything is ready for a quick start.

Basics

  • Use the Website app to build a beautiful website for your customers
  • Customers can book a session using the Appointments app's page on your website, and pay in just a few clicks
  • Appointments can be displayed and managed in the Calendar app .
  • Customers are hungry? Allow them to purchase drinks, snacks and a bite through the Point of Sale app
  • You can also manage in-shop bookings using the Point of Sale app, and the Appointments app

Included customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
