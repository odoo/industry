Odoo for Food & Beverage Distributor

This Odoo industry package is the essential ingredient for a successful food distribution business. It prioritizes freshness and safety by offering robust traceability tools, allowing you to track batches and expiration dates from the supplier to the customer. The solution streamlines your logistics, ensuring that perishable goods are moved quickly using FEFO (First Expired, First Out) strategies. By centralizing your inventory and sales data, it empowers you to reduce waste and maintain the highest standards of quality control.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Inventory App: Master your stock with advanced features like Lots & Serial Numbers for traceability and Expiration Dates to enforce FEFO (First Expired, First Out) removal strategies.
  • Sales App: Manage complex pricing structures with Pricelists, allowing you to offer specific deals to restaurants, retailers, or wholesalers.
  • Purchase App: Automate your procurement process with reordering rules that account for vendor lead times and seasonal demand spikes.
  • Fleet App: Manage your delivery trucks and vans, tracking fuel logs, maintenance schedules, and driver assignments in one place.
  • Invoicing App: Streamline your billing with automated invoicing based on delivered quantities, ensuring accurate financial records.
  • Documents App: Centralize supplier certificates, quality control reports, and shipping manifests for easy retrieval during audits.
