Odoo for Food Distribution

This industry package includes a set of data and is configured for you to run your food distribution business efficiently from Odoo.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Use the Manufacturing App to efficiently plan and execute production orders to meet customer demand.
  • Process manufacturing work orders with instructions from the Shopfloor App.
  • Manage the purchase of raw materials from the Purchase App.
  • From the Inventory App, keep track of produced lots and expiration dates.
  • Generate crystal clear quotations with the Sales App.
  • Invoice your customers from the Invoicing App.

Customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
