Solar Installation Business Management App

The Odoo app for solar installation professionals provides a preconfigured database with all the necessary features to manage their business. The app includes pre-configured products and projects, a website template, and professional quotes to help solar companies showcase their services and attract new clients. With this app, solar installation companies can easily manage their projects, track their time, and create invoices. The website template allows businesses to highlight their portfolio and services, and the pre-configured products make it easy to offer solar panels and related equipment to customers.
