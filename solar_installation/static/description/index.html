Odoo for Solar Energy Systems

This Odoo industry package radiates efficiency, designed specifically for solar installation professionals. It illuminates every stage of your business, from generating the initial lead to the final grid connection. The solution simplifies complex installations by managing project milestones, permitting, and equipment logistics in one system. By connecting your sales team with your field technicians, it ensures that what is promised to the customer is exactly what gets installed on the roof.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • CRM App: Capture leads from your website and track them through the sales funnel, managing site visits and technical feasibility checks.
  • Sales App: Create professional, detailed proposals that include technical specifications for panels, inverters, and labor costs.
  • Project App: Manage installation projects with clear stages (e.g., "Permitting," "Installation," "Inspection"), tracking tasks and deadlines.
  • Field Service App: Schedule your installation crews and maintenance teams efficiently, allowing them to log time and consume parts via mobile.
  • Inventory App: Keep track of high-value stock like solar modules and batteries, ensuring you have the right components for every job.
  • Website App: Showcase your portfolio of successful installations and attract new eco-conscious clients with a professional online presence.
  • Sign App: Securely collect digital signatures for contracts, interconnection agreements, and financing documents.

Customizations

  • Worksheets: Leverage Odoo Studio to design tailored Field Service Worksheets for every stage of the project, from capturing roof measurements during site surveys to validating safety checks during final commissioning.
