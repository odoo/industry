Odoo for Carpenter

This package is designed specifically for carpenters, providing all the essential tools you need to run your business efficiently.

Basics

  • Quoting: Create professional, appealing quotes, even for the most complex projects.
  • Project Management: Oversee every part of your carpentry jobs while tracking profitability to ensure a healthy bottom line.
  • Manufacturing: Generate all necessary sub-assemblies and production plans directly in your workshop.
  • Purchasing & Inventory: Keep track of all your material purchases and manage deliveries to both your shop and job sites.

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
