House Keeping Module

⚠️

The House Keeping Module is not part of Odoo standard but uses Odoo Studio and leverages lines of python code; therefore installing this module may increase your subscription.


Purpose

House Keeping Module aims at enabling the management and reporting of house keeping to support accommodation industries: Hotel, Campsite and Guest House.

It enables:

  • The monitoring of room cleanness status with a dedicated board;
  • The assignation and execution of tasks for house keeping per rooms.

Board

House Keeping menu is exposed in the accommodation apps and gives access to the board.

The board gives an overview of all resources involved to support stay offers with visual information to capture at first sight the cleanness status.

  • The occupancy captures the presence of guests for this resource.
    • Vacant: No guest.
    • Occupied: A guest checked in.
    • Stayover: Guest staying until the next day.
    • Due Out: Guest expected to check out today.
  • When a task is in progress for house keeping of this resource, the task name is provided as well as the assigned employee.
    The task stage is as well exposed.

Tasks

House Keeping menu is exposed in the accommodation apps and gives access to the tasks of the house keeping project.

The tasks give an overview of the work to be done for house keeping on a daily basis.

  • The cleaning provides the depth of cleaning expected.
    • Stayover: Guests are staying overnight so standard cleaning is needed.
    • Checkout: Guests are leaving so in depth cleaning is expected.
  • The occupancy captures the presence of guests for this resource.
Approvers

The settings enable to define approvers who can check and approve cleaning is complete for each room.
House keeping tasks listed in the Clean stage can be approved by one of the approvers listed in the settings.

💡

When the approvers setting is not set, house keeping tasks set to Clean stage automatically go to the Ready stage and get closed.

Process
Here is the typical flow the tasks follow to maintain house keeping up to date:
  1. At night, the house keeping tasks are created for rooms requiring cleaning thanks to a scheduled action.
  2. The house keeping tasks can be assigned and tackled by employees.
