Odoo for Shoemaker Services

This industry package includes all the essential features to manage your shoemaker services business, from customer relationship management to task and stock management as well as billing.

Basics

  • Leverage the Sales App to manage your quotations and orders.
  • Follow up on work completion with the Project App and Field Service App.
  • Easily bill customers with the Point of Sales App.
  • Slim your labor for stock management, replenishment and purchase with Inventory App and Purchase App.
  • Automatically notify your customers when their order are ready to collect.

Included Customizations

  • On SO turning to collectible, the customer is notified by email to collect the order.
