Odoo for Interior Design

This industry package includes all the essentials for managing your Interior Design firm, from design approval to work done, and website.

Basics

  • The Website App generates leads by showcasing your portfolio and allowing clients to reach out through a contact form.
  • The CRM App: captures and tracks potential clients, manages site visit scheduling, and monitors lead progress.
  • The Sales App: creates and manages quotations and sales orders for design projects.
  • The Project App: organizes and tracks design phases such as Mood Board, Visualization, and Handover.
  • The Timesheets App: tracks hours spent on creative design work for accurate costing and billing.
  • The Planning App: to assign the tasks and plan the work for the resources.
  • The Purchase App: manages vendor orders and procures furniture, materials, and fittings for projects.
  • The Document App: will have the designs shared with the customers at a centralised place.
  • We have added Site Visit Date and Property Type fields in the lead so that the person who is assigned to the lead will have a clear visibility of the type of the lead and when to visit the site.

Customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
