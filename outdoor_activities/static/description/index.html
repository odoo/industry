Odoo for Diving Tour Operators

This industry package includes everything you need to run your outdoor activities efficiently with Odoo. Whether you explore the depths of the oceans with your scuba diving school, soar through the clouds as a skydiving instructor or offer wild water adventures - this package is for you!

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • The Website App serves as your company's calling card, allows customers to contact you and even purchase their aquatic adventures from anywhere in the world.
  • TheCRM Appmanages all customer relations, helps you keep track of leads and website visitors and increase your sales.
  • Use the Sales Appto validate orders and create custom quotations for special VIP bookings.
  • With the Events Appyou can create certification courses and special Experiences for your customers.
  • The POS Appallows you to handle payments for walk-in customers.
  • Store and organize client information in the Contacts App.
  • Streamline billing and financial management with the Invoicing app.

Customisations

  • This industry relies on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
