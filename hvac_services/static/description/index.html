Odoo for HVAC services

This industry package is designed to run your HVAC services operations. It provides a first set of services, products, worksheets and reporting to install, maintain and repair HVAC devices on your customer sites with detailed traceability.

Basics

  • Create great quotations and manage your services in the Sales App.
  • Plan, follow and report your team work using the Field Service App.
  • Efficiently order your providers with the Purchase Apps.
  • Invoice in seconds and keep track of your payments in the Invoicing App.
  • Access devices history and related field services from the Contacts App.

Customizations

  • Relationship between field service tasks and serials with several automation's to be populated.
  • Relationship between subscriptions and serials based on serial input from maintenance order lines.
  • Reporting of refrigerant consumption and waste during field services.
