Odoo for Property Management

This Odoo industry package is the cornerstone of a professional property management operation. It is designed to streamline the management of rental portfolios on behalf of owners, covering everything from tenant onboarding to complex maintenance cycles. The solution centralizes lease agreements, owner reports, and facility management into one integrated ecosystem. By automating rent collection and maintenance dispatching, it empowers your team to maximize occupancy and preserve asset value without getting buried in administrative tasks.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • CRM App: Manage your leads efficiently, tracking ongoing buying and selling opportunities in a visual pipeline.
  • Subscriptions App: Automate the collection of recurring rent and service charges with flexible billing cycles, ensuring consistent cash flow and effortless lease renewals.
  • Sales App: Handle offers, negotiations, and closing deals with professional quotations and digital signatures.
  • Website App: Drive qualified traffic by promoting your listings on a beautiful, mobile-responsive property management portal.
  • Contacts App: Centralize all stakeholder data, managing the unique relationships between property owners, tenants, and service providers within a single, searchable directory with full communication history.
  • Properties App: Organize your portfolio with a dedicated app to manage property listings, contracts, and availability status.
