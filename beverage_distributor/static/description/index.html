Odoo for Beverage Distributor

This Odoo industry package is designed to quench the thirst for efficiency in beverage distribution. It addresses the unique challenges of the trade, such as managing complex bottle deposits and ensuring precise stock rotation. The solution integrates your warehouse operations with a robust sales engine, handling B2B orders and B2C point-of-sale transactions seamlessly. By automating the tracking of empties and taxes, it empowers you to maintain healthy margins while keeping your fleet and inventory moving fast.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Inventory App: Serve as the central hub for stock, managing consignments, batch tracking, and warehouse operations with precision.
  • Sales App: Handle high-volume client orders and manage complex product catalogs with varying units of measure (e.g., crate vs. bottle).
  • Point of Sale App: Process B2C sales efficiently at your distribution center's front counter or cash-and-carry section.
  • Purchase App: Automate replenishment from suppliers, ensuring you have the right mix of beverages to meet seasonal demand.
  • Barcode App: Accelerate picking and packing processes with mobile scanners, ensuring accurate inventory counts and faster loading.
  • eCommerce App: Expand your reach with a B2B portal that allows restaurants and retailers to place restocking orders online 24/7.
  • Manufacturing App: Manage the repackaging of bulk items into smaller saleable units automatically.

Customizations

  • Deposit Management: Utilize custom fields like Deposit Product and Empty Deposit Product to track and manage refundable container fees automatically.
  • Excise Management: Simplify compliance with specialized tools for Excise Management, ensuring accurate tax reporting.
  • Smart Replenishment: Trigger auto-creation of reordering rules for unit sale products, ensuring that stock is always available for packing.
  • Automated Repackaging: Automatically generate Bills of Materials (BoMs) that convert a single main unit (e.g., pallet) into the exact number of saleable units (e.g., bottles), with auto-production rules to streamline workflows.

Pricing

This module involves multiple apps including Studio for various views, fields and relationships. It as well relies on lines of code through server actions and computed fields to bring extra capabilities, which leads to maintenance fees.
