Odoo for Takeaway Restaurant

This Odoo industry package serves up a complete solution for running a busy takeaway business. It covers every step of the order lifecycle, from a customizable website for seamless online ordering to a robust in-store Point of Sale system. The solution integrates kitchen management tools to ensure orders are prepared quickly and accurately, while simultaneously tracking delivery progress. By connecting sales directly to inventory and purchasing, it helps you reduce food waste and keep your kitchen fully stocked with fresh ingredients.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Website App: Build an appetizing digital menu and enable customers to place orders online for pickup or delivery.
  • Point of Sale App: Register in-store orders quickly, processing payments and sending tickets directly to the kitchen.
  • POS Preparation Display: Replace paper tickets with a digital Kitchen Display System to manage order flow and preparation times efficiently.
  • Inventory App: Track ingredient usage in real-time to prevent stockouts and minimize food waste.
  • Purchase App: Manage supplier relationships and automate reordering for essential ingredients and packaging.
  • Invoicing App: Simplify your financial management by automatically tracking revenue from all sales channels.
