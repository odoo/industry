Odoo for Eyewear Stores

This industry package includes all the essential features for Eyewear stores, from eye test results record holding, to handling all possible sales channels and inventory management.

Basics

  • Sell your glasses and lenses through the eCommerce or in the shop.
  • Manage appointments for Eye tests.
  • Log your Eye test results in a visitor's contact sheet.
  • Keep track of your opportunities.
  • Manage your inventory, reorder products when stock is low.

Customizations

  • Added Lines to the contact sheets that contain various fields for record keeping of eye test results.
  • Added a custom report for sharing eye test results with a visitor.
