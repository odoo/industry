Odoo for Microbreweries

This industry package includes everything you need to run your company efficiently with Odoo.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Manage stock, consignments, and warehouse operations with the Inventory App.
  • Handle your production processes using the Manufacture App.
  • Streamline client orders and manage your product catalog with the Sales App.
  • Track leads, manage your sales pipeline, and nurture client relationships with the CRM.
  • Order from suppliers and manage your supply chain with the Purchase App.
  • Enhance your B2C sales process with the Point of Sale App.
  • Expand your business with a B2B/B2C eCommerce Website for online ordering.
  • Maintain a detailed database of clients and suppliers using the Contacts App.

Included Customizations

  • Additional fields on products to manage deposits, including:
    • Deposit Product
    • Quantity by Deposit Product
    • Unit Sale Product
    • Empty Deposit Product
  • Automatic addition of taxes from the Deposit Product to the main product.
  • Automatic creation of a "00" reordering rule for products selected in the Unit Sale Product field, with a manufacturing route.
  • Auto-creation of a Bill of Materials (BOM) based on the Unit Sale Product, allowing the specified quantity of the Unit Sale Product to be produced from one unit of the main product.
  • An additional Auto-production field on the BOM ensures that manufacturing orders for this BOM are automatically produced.
