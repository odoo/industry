Odoo for accounting firms

This industry package includes all the essential features to manage your accounting firm business, from order to customer relationship management as well as billing.

Basics

  • Use the CRM App and the Sales App to manage prospects, quotes and sales order.
  • Manage your work through Project App.
  • Invoice your customers with Accounting App.
  • Request and manage documents with Documents App.

Customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
