Odoo for Accounting Firm

This Odoo industry package provides a unified platform to manage the entire lifecycle of an accounting firm. It streamlines operations from client acquisition to final billing, ensuring that deadlines are met and client expectations exceeded. By consolidating customer relationship management, project tracking, and financial tools into one system, it reduces administrative overhead. This solution empowers firms to focus on delivering high-value financial advice rather than managing disjointed processes.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • CRM App & Sales App: Drive growth by managing opportunities, creating professional quotes, and confirming sales orders seamlessly.
  • Contacts App: Centralize all client data and maintain a clean, organized directory of customers and contacts.
  • Sign App: Accelerate approvals and agreements by capturing secure electronic document signatures instantly.
  • Project App: Organize client files, track tasks, and manage your team's workload to meet every deadline.
  • Timesheets App: Track billable hours with precision and analyze your team's efforts for better profitability.
  • Accounting App: Streamline your billing cycle and manage the firm's own financials with robust invoicing tools.
  • Equity App: Simplify compliance by gathering and visualizing Ultimate Beneficial Owners (UBOs) structures.
  • Databases App: Access and interact with your clients' Odoo databases instantly from a single dashboard.
  • Documents App: Automate the request, collection, and management of client files for a paperless office.

Customizations

  • Identity Documents: Securely store and retrieve Identity Documents directly from a dedicated tab on the person contact record.
  • Anti Money Laundering: Ensure full regulatory compliance by handling Anti Money Laundering duties within a dedicated contact tab.
  • Mission Details: Gain instant visibility into client engagements by capturing mission and recurrence details on the contact form as soon as a project is linked.

Pricing

This module involves multiple apps including Studio for various views, fields and relationships. It as well relies on lines of code through server actions and computed fields to bring extra capabilities, which leads to maintenance fees.
