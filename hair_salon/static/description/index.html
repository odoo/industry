Odoo for Hair Salon

This Odoo industry package provides a stylish solution for managing your hair salon. It is designed to cut through the administrative clutter, handling everything from client bookings to stock management for shampoos and dyes. The solution integrates a sleek Point of Sale with your appointment calendar, ensuring that walk-ins and pre-booked clients are handled with equal efficiency. By centralizing your operations, it allows your stylists to focus on their craft while the software manages the business side.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Appointments App: Allow clients to book specific services and stylists online, syncing directly with your team's calendar to prevent double-booking.
  • Point of Sale App: Process payments quickly at the chair or front desk, handling tips, gift cards, and product sales seamlessly.
  • Inventory App: Manage your stock of retail products (like shampoos and gels) and professional supplies (like color tubes and developers) in real-time.
  • Employees App: Manage specific stylist schedules and track attendance, ensuring you have the right talent available for peak hours.
  • Website App: Showcase your portfolio of cuts and colors, list your services, and attract new clients with a professional online presence.

Customizations

  • Optimized Scheduling: Save breaks with a dedicated input field within appointment types to automatically split bookings, freeing up staff schedules and optimizing slots for services of varying durations.

Pricing

This module involves multiple apps including Studio for various views, fields and relationships. It as well relies on lines of code through server actions and computed fields to bring extra capabilities, which leads to maintenance fees.
