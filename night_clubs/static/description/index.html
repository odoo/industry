Odoo for Night Clubs

This industry package includes all the essentials for managing your night club from selling tickets to drinks, managing your social media presence, and more!

Basics

  • Website : Sell event tickets online and deliver confirmations with QR codes.
  • Events : Manage schedules, bookings, and attendee check-ins.
  • Point of Sale : Sell tickets or drinks at the entrance and bar with seamless payments.
  • Social Marketing : Boost visibility with integrated campaigns on Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and X.
  • Purchase : Automate supplier management and resupply of drinks and consumables.

Customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
