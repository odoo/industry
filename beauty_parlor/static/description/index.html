Odoo for Beauty Parlor

This industry package is designed specifically for beauty parlors, institutes, and nail studios. It helps you manage your clients, treatments, staff schedules, and payments in one fully integrated system; from online booking to selling services or products directly at the front desk.

Basics

  • Appointments App: Offer online booking for treatments and services so your clients can schedule sessions 24/7.
  • CRM App: Track client interactions, preferences, and follow-ups all in one central hub to nurture long-term relationships.
  • Calendar App: Manage your daily schedule efficiently, avoid double bookings, and ensure enough preparation time between clients.
  • Invoicing App: Generate professional invoices for treatments, packages, and subscriptions, making sure you get paid on time.
  • Sales App: Sell treatment packages, gift cards, and retail products with clear quotations and pricing.
  • Point of Sale App: Let clients pay at the front desk and purchase retail products immediately after their treatments.
  • Contacts App: Keep a clean and organized directory of your clients, suppliers, and professional partners.

Customizations

  • This industry package relies entirely on standard Odoo features and does not include any custom developments.
