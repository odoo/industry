Odoo for Spa Resort

This Odoo industry package is designed to create a tranquil and efficient environment for spa resorts of all sizes. Whether you offer day passes, deep tissue massages, or full wellness retreats, it manages the entire guest journey in one place. The solution integrates online booking with staff scheduling and inventory, ensuring that treatments are booked smoothly and oils are always in stock. By streamlining operations, it allows your team to focus entirely on delivering a relaxing and rejuvenating experience.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Appointment App: Manage bookings for spa entrances and massages with a clear, flexible schedule that prevents double-booking.
  • Point of Sale App: Upsell relaxation by selling tickets, cosmetics, and accessories like bathrobes directly at the reception.
  • Sales App: Handle complex bookings like group offers and special spa packages by sending professional quotations.
  • Website & eCommerce App: Showcase your wellness menu online, allowing guests to book treatments and buy gift cards 24/7.
  • Planning App: Organize your therapists' schedules efficiently to match staff availability with treatment bookings.
  • Inventory App: Monitor the consumption of wellness products like essential oils and towels to ensure you are always prepared.
