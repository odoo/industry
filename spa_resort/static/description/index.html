Odoo for Spa Resort

This set of applications is built for spa resorts of all sizes; whether you’re offering day passes, relaxing massages, or wellness retreats. Odoo helps you manage everything in one place: online bookings, massages and  treatments, point of sales, gift cards, staff planning, and guest experiences.

Basics

  • Appointment: Manage bookings for spa entrance and massages with a clear and flexible schedule.
  • Point of Sale (POS): Sell tickets, accessories, or cosmetics directly on-site.
  • Sales: Send quotations, confirm group offers, and handle special spa packages.
  • Website & eCommerce: Showcase your wellness offers and allow online bookings.
  • Planning: Schedule staff for entrances and treatments.
  • Inventory: Monitor wellness products like oils, cosmetics, and accessories (bathrobes, slippers,...).

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
