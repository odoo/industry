Odoo for Bookstore

This industry package includes everything you need to run your bookstore efficiently with Odoo.

Basics

  • Use the Point of Sale for your sales at the desk. Manage your customer accounts and advantages in the Contacts App.
  • Use the Sales App to order books for your customers, create quotations for your institutional clients (libraries, schools, etc.), and invoice them on the go using the Invoicing App.
  • Use the Purchase App to order books from your distributors.
  • Use the Inventory App to manage your orders and receive and deliver your products.
  • Use the Barcode App to scan your products and create new entries directly from this App to encode new books faster than ever.

Included customizations

  • Reordering rules with manual triggers are automatically created at product creation to ensure appearance in the Replenishment report.
  • The "Last sale" field records each product's last picking-out date.
  • The "Last 7 days" filter in the Replenishment report provides an exhaustive view of sold books from the week, even if they are still on hand.
  • A "Vendor" is automatically set and updated based on contact from receipt. An alert appears if a receipt doesn't have a contact.
  • "Cost field" and "Product category field" are available in Barcode when you add a new product.
  • A Purchase Order is automatically created when you create a receipt without a source document.
  • At each change, the sale price is automatically added to the "Description for receipt" field on the product so it can be displayed in the Barcode App.
