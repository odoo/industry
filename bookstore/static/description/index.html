Odoo for Book Store

This Odoo industry package writes a new chapter in efficiency for your bookstore. It is designed to manage thousands of titles with ease, allowing you to track inventory by ISBN and category. The solution integrates a fast Point of Sale system with your back-office, ensuring that every sale instantly updates your stock levels. By streamlining purchasing and returns, it empowers you to curate a collection that delights your readers while keeping your business profitable.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Point of Sale App: Process transactions quickly using barcode scanners to read ISBNs, handling discounts and customer loyalty programs with ease.
  • Inventory App: Master your library of titles, using barcode scanning for rapid stock adjustments and managing locations for different genres or sections.
  • Purchase App: Automate replenishment orders based on sales velocity, ensuring you never run out of the latest bestsellers.
  • eCommerce App: Expand your readership by selling books online, offering "click and collect" options or shipping directly to readers' homes.
  • Invoicing App: Simplify your bookkeeping by tracking daily sales and supplier invoices in one integrated financial system.
  • Barcode App: Use mobile scanners to perform inventory counts and receive new shipments efficiently, reducing manual entry errors.

Customizations

  • Replenishment Visibility: Automatically create reordering rules with manual triggers for every new product to ensure they appear in the Replenishment report.
  • Sales Tracking: Track the pulse of your inventory with a "Last sale" field that records the date of the last picking for each book.
  • Smart Filtering: Use the "Last 7 days" filter in the Replenishment report to get an exhaustive view of recently sold books, even if they are still in stock.
  • Vendor Automation: Automatically set and update the Vendor based on the contact from the receipt, with intelligent alerts if a contact is missing.
  • Barcode Enhancements: View essential data like Cost and Product Category directly in the Barcode App when adding a new book to your catalog.
  • Workflow Automation: Automatically generate a Purchase Order whenever a receipt is created without an existing source document.
  • Price Display: Automatically add the sale price to the "Description for receipt" field so it is clearly displayed within the Barcode App.

Pricing

This module involves multiple apps including Studio for various views, fields and relationships. It as well relies on lines of code through server actions and computed fields to bring extra capabilities, which leads to maintenance fees.
