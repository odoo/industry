Odoo for Museums

This industry package is designed specifically for institutions in the museum sector—whether you're running a historical museum, a contemporary art gallery, or a science and discovery center. Odoo brings everything you need to manage ticket sales, memberships, exhibitions, artwork inventory, shops, and staff coordination in a single integrated platform.

Basics

  • Use the Events app to manage entry slots, ticket types, and attendance.
  • Promote exhibitions and sell tickets online using Website and eCommerce.
  • Handle on-site ticket and gift shop sales through the Point of Sale system.
  • Automate membership renewals and manage benefits with the Subscriptions app.
  • Track artworks and their stock locations using the Inventory app.
  • Place vendor orders for artwork acquisitions or retail items using Purchase.
  • Digitally sign artist contracts and supplier agreements with Sign.
  • Organize staff schedules across departments with the Planning app—from front desk staff to gallery attendants.

Customisations

  • This industry relies on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
