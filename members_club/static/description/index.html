Odoo for Club Members

This industry package includes everything for a modern members club looking to manage memberships, subscriptions, events, community engagement & website.

Basics

  • The CRM App helps you manage membership inquiries and partnerships.
  • The Membership & Subscription Apps track members and automate renewals.
  • The Ecommerce App lets you sell memberships and club products online.
  • The Event App helps you organize unforgettable experiences for your members.
  • The Email Marketing & Social Marketing Apps engage your community across channels.
  • The Website Apps helps you build a professional digital home for your club.

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
