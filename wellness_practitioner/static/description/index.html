Odoo for Wellness Practitioner

This Odoo industry package is designed to bring balance and efficiency to wellness practitioners. It offers a complete solution to manage the core aspects of your practice, from scheduling appointments to maintaining strong client relationships. The system supports you in delivering personalized care by organizing treatment plans and patient notes in one secure location. By streamlining billing and administrative tasks, it allows you to focus wholeheartedly on the holistic health and well-being of your clients.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Appointments App: Offer seamless online booking for consultations and sessions, allowing clients to schedule time with you 24/7.
  • CRM App: Nurture long-term relationships by tracking client interactions, preferences, and follow-up reminders in one central hub.
  • Knowledge App: Securely store and organize detailed patient notes, treatment plans, and holistic health records for easy reference.
  • Calendar App: Manage your daily schedule efficiently, avoiding double bookings and ensuring you have adequate preparation time between clients.
  • Invoicing App: Simplify your finances by generating professional invoices for sessions and packages, ensuring you get paid on time.
  • Contacts App: Maintain a clean and organized directory of all your clients, suppliers, and professional partners.
