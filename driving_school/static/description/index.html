Odoo for Driving School

This industry package is tailored for driving schools of all sizes—whether you run a local training center or a large-scale operation. Odoo helps you manage everything from lesson scheduling and vehicle tracking to staff planning and online bookings—all in one seamless platform. By centralizing your operations, it ensures that your vehicles are maintained, your instructors are booked efficiently, and your students have a smooth path to getting their license.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Sales App: Sell lessons, exams, and course packages directly to students, managing invoices and payments in one place.
  • Sign App: Handle legal waivers and student agreements digitally, ensuring all necessary documentation is signed and stored securely.
  • Appointments App: Allow students to book lessons online 24/7, syncing automatically with instructor availability to prevent double-booking.
  • Planning App: Manage instructors' schedules efficiently, assigning shifts and ensuring the right teacher is available for the right class.
  • Fleet App: Track and maintain your vehicles, monitoring fuel logs, service contracts, and odometer readings to keep your cars road-ready.
  • Website App: Boost your school's visibility and manage your site, creating a professional portal for new students to register and learn about your services.
