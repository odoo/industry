Odoo for Driving Schools

This industry package is tailored for driving schools of all sizes—whether you run a local training center or a large-scale operation. Odoo helps you manage everything from lesson scheduling and vehicle tracking to staff planning and online bookings—all in one seamless platform.

Basics

  • Sell lessons, exams, and course packages using the Sales app.
  • Handle legal waivers and agreements with Sign.
  • Allow students to book lessons online with the Appointments app.
  • Manage instructors' schedules using the Planning app.
  • Track and maintain vehicles with the Fleet app.
  • Boost your school's visibility and manage your site through the Website app.

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
