Odoo for Hotel

This Odoo industry package is the perfect host for your hospitality business. It centralizes every aspect of guest management, from seamless online bookings to the final checkout invoice. The solution integrates your room inventory with a powerful planning tool, ensuring that housekeeping and front desk staff are always synchronized. By automating administrative tasks and billing, it empowers you to focus on what truly matters: providing a warm and memorable stay for your guests.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Website & eCommerce App: Showcase your rooms and amenities online, enabling customers to book directly through a professional, mobile-friendly portal.
  • Rental App: Manage room availability and pricing models, ensuring that every booking is tracked and double-bookings are impossible.
  • Planning App: Organize housekeeping and front desk shifts, binding them to rental orders to ensure rooms are ready for new arrivals.
  • Invoicing App: Simplify billing by generating professional invoices that include room charges, extra services, and automatically computed city taxes.
  • Contacts App: Record guest identities securely, linking them to their rental orders for compliance and personalized service.
  • Discuss App: Facilitate communication between staff members, ensuring that maintenance requests or special guest needs are shared instantly.

Customizations

  • Booking Management: Manage planning, check-in, and check-out directly from the Hotel App interface.
  • Gantt View Integration: Visualize interactions between Rental orders and Planning shifts to manage bookings and room preparation efficiently.
  • City Tax: Automatically compute city tax based on the number of booked rooms and nights within the Rental order.
  • Guest Identity: Record guest identity details in the Contacts App and relate them directly to their specific Rental orders for security and reporting.
  • Customer Account: Easily balance customer accounts for expenses from PoS at the bar and restaurant from their invoices.
  • House keeping module provides a dedicated board and tasks.

Pricing

This module involves multiple apps including Studio for various views, fields and relationships. It as well relies on lines of code through server actions and computed fields to bring extra capabilities, which leads to maintenance fees.
