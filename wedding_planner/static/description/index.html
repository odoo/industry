Odoo for Wedding Planners

This industry package includes all the essential apps and configurations for Wedding Planners to organise their business, follow-up with customers, and plan weddings.

Basics

  • Use the CRM App to track your leads and prospects.
  • Create beautiful quotations through the Sales App.
  • Manage Weddings, and everything surrounding it through the Projects App.
  • Manage contracts & agreements through the Sign App.
  • Invoice your customer in seconds through the Invoices App.

Customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
