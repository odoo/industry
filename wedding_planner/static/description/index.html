Odoo for Wedding Planner

This Odoo industry package is the ultimate companion for wedding planners dedicated to creating unforgettable moments. It centralizes every detail of the planning process, from the initial consultation to the final toast on the big day. The solution allows you to meticulously organize timelines, manage vendor contracts, and track budgets in real-time. By streamlining the logistics, it empowers you to focus on the creative design and emotional experience of your couples.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • CRM App: Capture the details of engaged couples and manage the sales pipeline from the first inquiry to the signed contract.
  • Project App: Create detailed planning checklists and timelines, ensuring every task—from cake tasting to venue booking—is completed on schedule.
  • Sales App: Create beautiful, itemized quotations for different wedding packages and manage down payments easily.
  • Contacts App: Maintain a comprehensive directory of trusted vendors, venues, and suppliers to quickly connect them with your clients.
  • Invoicing App: Keep the budget on track by generating professional invoices for your services and tracking vendor payments.
  • Documents App: Centralize contracts, mood boards, and floor plans in one secure location for easy sharing and retrieval.
  • Events App: Orchestrate the flow of the wedding day itself, managing schedules and on-site logistics with precision.
