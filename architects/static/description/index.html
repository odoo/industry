Odoo for Architecture Firm

Elevate your architecture practice with this specialized Odoo industry package. From initial design development to final worksite management, this solution provides the tools you need to succeed. It seamlessly integrates project management, time recording, and CRM, giving you a 360-degree view of your business. The setup is designed to streamline your operations, allowing your team to focus on creativity and client satisfaction rather than administrative tasks.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Project App: Orchestrate complex architectural projects by managing distinct phases like schematic design, development, and construction.
  • Timesheets App: Ensure profitability by accurately tracking time spent on design drafting, client meetings, and site visits.
  • CRM App: Centralize client interactions to effectively manage leads and convert design proposals into signed contracts.
  • Documents App: Securely organize, version, and share critical files such as architectural drawings, permits, and blueprints.
  • Sales App: Create professional proposals and contracts tailored for architectural services, streamlining the agreement process.
  • Invoicing App: Maintain healthy cash flow by billing clients flexibly based on project milestones or actual time spent.
  • Knowledge App: Centralize design standards, building codes, and internal guidelines to ensure consistency across the team.
