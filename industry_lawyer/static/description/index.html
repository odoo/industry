Odoo for Handyman Services

This industry provides a comprehensive suite of tools designed specifically for law firms, enabling efficient management of time, appointments, deadlines, cases, and client relationships.

Lawyers represent their clients and protect their interests during legal proceedings.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • The Calendar App manages schedules and appointments.
  • Manage client assessment with the CRM App and handles quotations and contracts with the Sales App.
  • The Timesheet App tracks billable hours and time spent.
  • Use the Sign App to securely sign engagement letters and legal documents, ensuring clear agreements before or after legal work.
  • Ease the invoicing process with the Sign App.
  • Streamline legal research and collaboration with the Knowledge App by centralizing case law, legal texts, and internal guides for efficient retrieval, collaboration, and compliance.

Included customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
