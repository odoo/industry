Odoo for Furniture Store

This Odoo industry package is designed to handle the fast-paced environment of grocery retail. It optimizes your operations for high turnover, ensuring that checkout lines move quickly and shelves are always restocked. The solution integrates a robust Point of Sale with advanced inventory tracking, allowing you to manage expiration dates and fresh produce efficiently. By connecting your supply chain directly to your sales data, it minimizes waste and maximizes the freshness of the goods you offer.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Point of Sale App: Process transactions rapidly with an interface optimized for speed, supporting barcode scanners, integrated scales, and customer loyalty cards.
  • Inventory App: Manage stock with precision using Lots & Serial Numbers and Expiration Dates to track perishables and automate removal strategies.
  • Barcode App: Empower staff to perform stock counts, receive goods, and check prices on the shop floor using mobile scanners.
  • Purchase App: Automate the reordering of fast-moving goods based on minimum stock rules, ensuring you never run out of milk or bread.
  • Invoicing App: Simplify your financial workflows by integrating supplier bills and customer invoices directly into your accounting system.
  • eCommerce App: Expand your reach by offering online ordering for "click and collect" or home delivery services.
