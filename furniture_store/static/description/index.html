Odoo for Furniture Stores

This module optimizes your furniture store's daily operations by streamlining product sales, customer orders, and the management of both in-store inventory and your website's online catalog.

Odoo offers a complete solution to enhance efficiency and improve customer satisfaction across all your furniture store services.

Basics

  • Use the Point of Sale app for quick and easy in-store transactions;
  • Leverage the eCommerce app to set up an online storefront, showcasing your entire toy catalog and allowing customers to shop 24/7;
  • Employ the Inventory app to manage your stock levels, ensuring furniture's are always available;
  • Utilize the Barcorde app for seamless inventory management and quick product lookups;
  • Use the Purchase app to reorder products from suppliers and manage vendor relationships.

Included customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
