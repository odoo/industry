Odoo for Cleaning Services

This industry package includes all the essentials for a cleaning services company, from opportunity management, to field service, and invoicing.

Basics

  • The CRM App manages customer inquiries and tracks leads with efficient follow-ups.
  • The Sales App creates quotations and sales orders, converting leads into transactions.
  • The Field Service Tasks dispatches on-site cleaning tasks promptly upon order confirmation.
  • The Invoicing App automates bill generation and tracks payments for seamless financial management.
  • The Recurring Visits schedules regular cleaning visits automatically for consistent service delivery in the field service app.

Included customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
