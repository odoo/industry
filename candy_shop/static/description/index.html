Odoo for Candy Shops

This package is ideal for any candy shop—whether you specialize in handcrafted chocolates, classic hard candies, or gourmet confections. We will help you attract sweet-toothed customers, manage inventory and suppliers, and conveniently sell both in-store and online. This guide will walk you through how to efficiently run your candy business with Odoo’s integrated tools.

Basics

  • The Website & eCommerce Apps create an online storefront where customers can browse and order their favorite sweets.
  • The Point of Sale App processes in-store transactions quickly, including support for weight-based pricing.
  • The Inventory App tracks stock levels, manages reorders, and prevents shortages.
  • The Purchase App manages vendor relationships and automates replenishment of popular products.
  • The Product Attributes & Categories use brands, flavors, and types to help customers find what they love.

Customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
