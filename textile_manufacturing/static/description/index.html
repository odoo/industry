Odoo for Textile Manufacturing

This Odoo industry package weaves together every aspect of textile production into a seamless digital fabric. It is designed to handle diverse manufacturing workflows, ranging from standard Make-to-Stock operations to intricate bespoke designs and client collaborations. The solution connects your entire supply chain, linking project management for new collections directly to replenishment, subcontracting, and final delivery. By centralizing these processes, it ensures you can execute complex production runs with precision and speed.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • CRM App & Sales App: Manage client relationships and opportunities, creating stunning quotations for new collections or bulk orders.
  • Project App: Collaborate on pre-sale design projects and custom requests, tracking progress from concept to approval.
  • Manufacturing App: Level up your production processes, managing Bills of Materials (BoMs) and work centers efficiently.
  • Shop Floor App: Empower your production team with a digital interface to track time, components, and work orders in real-time.
  • Inventory App: Fasten your purchasing cycles and track raw materials across your warehouse and subcontractor locations.
  • Invoicing App: Streamline your cash flow by invoicing clients at the right time, whether upon order confirmation or delivery.
