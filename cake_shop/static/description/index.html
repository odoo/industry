Odoo for Cake Shop

This setup is tailored for cake shop companies selling directly to consumers. Cake shops are businesses that specialize in offering a variety of cakes, pastries, and other confectionery items, catering to personalized and special orders for occasions like birthdays, weddings, and celebrations.

Basics

  • The Purchase App manages raw material orders and vendors.
  • The Point of Sale App manages in-store orders and payments.
  • The Sales App streamlines customer orders and quotations, such as managing custom cake requests.
  • The Website & eCommerce Apps enables online sales.

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
