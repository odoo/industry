Odoo for Real Estate Agencies

This industry package includes all the essential features to manage your real estate business, from lead tracking to property matchmaking and appointment scheduling.

Basics

  • Use the CRM App to manage your leads and track ongoing buying and selling opportunities.
  • Let clients book property visits easily through the Appointments App.
  • Handle offers, negotiations, and deals with the Sales App.
  • Manage your property listings in a dedicated Properties App.
  • Drive qualified traffic and promote listings through your Website.

Customizations

  • Matchmaking between potential buyers and properties for sale.
  • Notification emails sent regularly to update potential buyers about new property matches.
  • Automatic property assignment on lead creation from emails using external references.
  • Dynamic form to assign leads to the correct property directly from product pages.
  • Adapted product form to better reflect real estate data.
  • Automated creation of appointment types with the ability to send booking links.
