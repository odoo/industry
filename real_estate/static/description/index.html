Odoo for Real Estate Agency

This Odoo industry package is the key to unlocking a more efficient real estate business. It is designed to streamline the entire property lifecycle, from capturing leads to closing deals. The solution centralizes your listings, client requirements, and agent schedules into one dynamic platform. By automating property matchmaking and follow-ups, it empowers your agents to focus on building relationships and selling homes rather than managing paperwork.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • CRM App: Manage your leads efficiently, tracking ongoing buying and selling opportunities in a visual pipeline.
  • Properties App: Organize your portfolio with a dedicated app to manage property listings, details, and availability status.
  • Appointments App: Allow clients to book property visits and consultations directly with agents, synchronizing calendars automatically.
  • Sales App: Handle offers, negotiations, and closing deals with professional quotations and digital signatures.
  • Website App: Drive qualified traffic by promoting your listings on a beautiful, mobile-responsive real estate portal.

Customizations

  • Property Matchmaking: Automatically link potential buyers with properties for sale based on their preferences and criteria.
  • Automated Notifications: Send notification emails regularly to update potential buyers about new property matches that fit their needs.
  • Lead Assignment: Ensure automatic property assignment on lead creation from emails using external references to route inquiries correctly.
  • Dynamic Forms: Utilize a dynamic form to assign leads to the correct property directly from product pages on your website.

Pricing

This module involves multiple apps including Studio for various views, fields and relationships. It as well relies on lines of code through server actions and computed fields to bring extra capabilities, which leads to maintenance fees.
