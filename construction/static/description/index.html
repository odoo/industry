Tailored Setup for Construction Businesses

This industry is ideal for construction businesses proficient in managing projects from conception to completion, focusing on accurate quoting, efficient planning, seamless execution, and excellent customer service. The setup integrates all necessary modules (Project, Inventory, Sales, Purchase, ...) to streamline your operations, with the possibility to expand into advanced functionalities using additional applications.

Basics

  • CRM - Capture leads, schedule follow-ups, and track customer communications.
  • Sales - Create quotations, manage approvals, and convert quotes to orders.
  • Documents - Store contracts, designs, and project documentation in one central location.
  • Projects - Plan tasks, assign resources, and track progress on construction projects.
  • Inventory - Monitor stock, manage materials, and track deliveries to sites.
  • Planning - Schedule tasks, allocate employees, and coordinate work efficiently.
  • Timesheets - Log work hours, track productivity, and measure project progress.
  • Accounting & Invoicing - Generate invoices, manage payments, and reconcile accounts.
  • Helpdesk - Provide customer support and track service requests.
  • Field Service - Plan on-site tasks with detailed worksheet when needed.

Included customizations

  • Progress management - Easily handle sale orders and project progress in a dedicated view.
  • Quotation line numbering - Save time iterating on lengthy quotes.
  • Work items - Send your first quote in record time thanks to work items and fine tune your margin per service.
