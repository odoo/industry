Odoo for Construction

Build your business on a solid foundation with this Odoo industry package. It offers a comprehensive solution to manage the entire construction lifecycle, from the first estimate to the final handover. The system centralizes project management, allowing you to track tasks, materials, and labor costs in real-time. By connecting your site managers with the back office, it ensures that blueprints, permits, and daily logs are always accessible, keeping your projects on schedule and within budget.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Project App: Manage construction sites as projects, organizing tasks into stages like "Foundation," "Framing," and "Finishing" with clear deadlines.
  • Timesheets App: Track labor hours accurately with a mobile app that allows workers to log time against specific tasks or cost codes on-site.
  • Field Service App: Schedule and dispatch specialized crews for specific interventions, tracking their location and job status in real-time.
  • Purchase App: Automate the procurement of building materials, managing RFQs and purchase orders to ensure timely delivery to the site.
  • Inventory App: Keep track of tools, equipment, and consumables across multiple sites or warehouses, reducing loss and theft.
  • Invoicing App: Handle complex billing scenarios like milestone invoicing, down payments, and retention based on project progress.
  • Documents App: Centralize blueprints, contracts, and permits, ensuring that the latest versions are always available to the team.
  • Fleet App: Manage your heavy machinery and vehicle fleet, tracking maintenance schedules, fuel costs, and driver assignments.
