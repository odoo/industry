Odoo for Restaurant

This Odoo industry package serves up a Michelin-star operational experience for your restaurant. It is designed to streamline the chaotic flow of a busy dining room, from table reservations to the final bill split. The solution integrates your front-of-house POS with the kitchen, ensuring that orders are fired instantly and accurately. By centralizing inventory and staff scheduling, it empowers you to focus on culinary excellence and hospitality while the software handles the heat of the service.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Restaurant App (POS): Manage your floor plan visually, handling table seating, order taking, and bill splitting directly from the interface.
  • Kitchen Display / Printers: Send orders instantly to the kitchen or bar with specific printing instructions for different stations (e.g., "Cold Station", "Grill").
  • Appointments App: Allow customers to book tables online 24/7, reducing phone calls and optimizing your seating capacity.
  • Inventory App: Track ingredients and recipes in real-time, using "Bill of Materials" (BoM) to deduct stock automatically as dishes are sold.
  • Planning App: Organize shifts for chefs, servers, and bartenders, ensuring you are adequately staffed for peak service hours.
  • Website App: Showcase your menu, share chef bios, and promote special events with a mouth-watering online presence.
  • Purchase App: Automate the reordering of fresh produce and dry goods based on minimum stock rules and supplier lead times.
