Odoo for Wine Merchant

This industry package includes everything a small wine merchant needs to manage sales, purchases, inventory, events, and an online store.

Basics

  • The CRM App manages client interactions and tracks potential leads for future sales opportunities.
  • The Sales App streamlines the creation of quotations and sales orders for efficient transaction handling.
  • The Point of Sale App enables seamless in-store wine sales through an intuitive POS interface.
  • The Ecommerce App showcases your wine selection and allows customers to purchase products online with ease.
  • The Event App lets customers register for and participate in wine-related events and tastings.
  • The Inventory App helps you manage wine stock and warehouse operations with structure and clarity.
  • The Purchase App tracks and manages your wine purchase orders from suppliers.

Included customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
