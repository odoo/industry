Odoo for Wine Shop

Toast to efficiency with this all-encompassing Odoo solution tailored for wine merchants. It simplifies the management of diverse wine stocks, ensuring you have clear visibility over vintages, regions, and suppliers. The integrated system connects your point of sale, eCommerce, and CRM, allowing you to nurture relationships with your customers and track their preferences. Whether you are hosting a tasting event or shipping a case across the country, this package provides the structure and clarity needed to grow your business.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • CRM App: Cultivate lasting relationships with wine lovers by managing interactions and tracking potential leads for private sales or memberships.
  • Sales App: Streamline your B2B and B2C transactions by creating professional quotations and sales orders efficiently.
  • Point of Sale App: Deliver a smooth in-store experience with an intuitive interface that handles bottle and case sales seamlessly.
  • eCommerce App: Expand your reach beyond the shop floor by showcasing your wine selection and enabling secure online purchases.
  • Events App: Engage your community by organizing and managing registrations for wine tastings, workshops, and exclusive release parties.
  • Inventory App: Manage your cellar with precision, ensuring structure and clarity for thousands of bottles, vintages, and locations.
  • Purchase App: Automate the replenishment of your stock by tracking purchase orders and managing supplier relationships effectively.
