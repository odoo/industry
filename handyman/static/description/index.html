Odoo for Handyman Services

This industry package includes all the essential features to manage your handyman business, from customer relationship management to field service operations and invoicing.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Use the CRM App to track your leads and prospects.
  • Create great quotations and manage your products in the Sales App.
  • Follow your interventions using the Field Service App.
  • Track your inventory and your purchases in the related Inventory and Purchase Apps.
  • Invoice in seconds and keep track of your payments in the Invoicing App.

Included Customizations

  • Add a link from vendor bills to tasks for easy tracking and management.
  • Add a button to create vendor bills directly from a field service task, and link the vendor bills with the task.
  • Add a smart button on tasks to view related vendor bills at a glance.
  • Add a computed field on tasks to show the total amount of vendor bills registered for the task.
  • Introduce a progress field on tasks that computes the consumption between the materials budget and the invoices, providing better financial oversight.
