Odoo for Holiday Houses

This industry package includes all the essential features to manage your holiday house business, from online booking to guest relationship management and house management as well as billing.

Basics

  • Online booking from eCommerce / Website App for your customers.
  • Leverage Rental services and Planning roles binding to prevent double booking.

Customizations

  • Manage plan, check in and check out your bookings from Holiday House App.
  • Interactions between Rental orders and Planning shifts to manage booking from a Gantt view.
  • City tax pre-computed based on nights from Rental order lines.
  • Guest identity recorded in Contacts App and related to Rental orders.
