Odoo for Sport Events

This industry includes organizing a wide range of sports events such as marathons, trail runs, triathlons, and competitive tournaments. It covers everything from event planning and vendor coordination to managing sponsorships, inventory, ticket sales, and staff scheduling.

Basics

  • Events: Plan and manage sport competitions, races, or tournaments.
  • Website: Promote events, sell tickets, and gather sponsorships online.
  • Inventory: Track sports gear, event equipment, merchandise, and manage stock of all materials.
  • Purchase: Order ravitaillement supplies, gear, equipment, and other essentials for sport events.
  • CRM: Manage relationships with sponsors, partners, and key contacts.
  • Project: Organize event planning, deadlines, and task responsibilities.
  • Planning: Create staff schedules, assign roles, and manage shifts.
  • Sign: Digitally sign sponsorship agreements or volunteer forms.
  • Survey: Gather feedback from participants, staff, and sponsors.

Customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
