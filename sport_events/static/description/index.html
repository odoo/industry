Odoo for Sport Events

This Odoo industry package is the champion of event management, designed to handle everything from local tournaments to major athletic competitions. It centralizes the entire organization process, allowing you to manage team registrations, schedule matches, and sell tickets online seamlessly. The solution integrates powerful communication tools to keep athletes and fans informed. By streamlining the logistics, it empowers you to deliver a flawless experience on game day.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Events App: Orchestrate tournaments and matches with ease, handling participant registrations, ticketing, and capacity management online.
  • Website App: Build a dedicated event portal to publish schedules, results, and venue information for fans and athletes.
  • eCommerce App: Boost revenue by selling event merchandise, gear, and concessions directly through your event website.
  • Email Marketing App: Keep the excitement high by sending automated updates, newsletters, and reminders to attendees and participants.
  • Contacts App: Maintain a database of teams, athletes, sponsors, and volunteers to streamline communication and coordination.
  • Project App: Manage the logistical setup of the event, assigning tasks to staff and volunteers to ensure the venue is ready on time.
