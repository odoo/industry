Odoo for Theater

This industry package includes everything you need to run your community theater with Odoo.

Basics

  • Use the Events app to manage performances and ticket sales.
  • Use the Point of Sale app to sell drinks and snacks at the bar.
  • Use the CRM app to track contacts, especially for fundraising and seasonal passes.
  • Use the Website and eCommerce apps to showcase your theater and sell tickets online.
  • Use the Email Marketing app to promote upcoming shows and communicate with your audience.

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
