Odoo for Summer Camps Organizers

This industry package includes all the essentials for the organization of Summer Camps.

Basics

  • Website 🌐 : Showcase your camps online with dedicated pages, integrated registration, mobile-friendly design, and secure payment—all from a visually engaging and parent-friendly website.

  • Events 🏨 : Create and manage every camp session with custom forms, pricing, capacity, tags, attendee tracking, automated emails, and a live check-in desk—all in one central app.

  • Projects 📋 : Organize the behind-the-scenes work of each camp using task lists, deadlines, assignments, and logistics tracking to ensure smooth preparation and coordination.

  • Appointments 📅 : Let families book meetings to discuss camp options, sync your team’s calendars, and reduce no-shows with automatic reminders and structured scheduling.

  • CRM 🎯 :Capture inquiries from your website, organize and qualify leads in a visual pipeline, assign sales tasks, and convert interest into bookings with quotations and notes tracking.

  • Sign ✍️ : Collect parental consent securely with reusable digital signature templates, embedded links in confirmation emails, and centralized access to signed documents.

Customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
