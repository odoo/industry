Odoo for Summer Camps

This Odoo industry package is the ultimate toolkit for camp organizers, bringing registration, scheduling, and parent communication into one seamless platform. It simplifies the chaos of camp management by centralizing event creation, attendee tracking, and secure payments. The solution also streamlines the collection of parental consent forms through digital signatures, ensuring compliance and peace of mind. By automating administrative tasks, it allows you to focus on creating unforgettable experiences for the campers.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Website App: Showcase your camps online with mobile-friendly pages, integrated registration, and secure payment processing to boost enrollments.
  • Events App: Create and manage every camp session with custom forms, capacity tracking, and automated emails to keep parents informed.
  • Projects App: Organize the behind-the-scenes logistics using task lists and deadlines to ensure every activity is prepared to perfection.
  • Appointments App: Let families book meetings to discuss camp options easily, reducing no-shows with automatic reminders.
  • CRM App: Capture inquiries from your website, qualify leads in a visual pipeline, and convert interest into confirmed bookings.
  • Sign App: Collect parental consent securely with reusable digital signature templates, ensuring all legal documents are signed and stored centrally.
