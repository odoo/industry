Odoo for Land Surveyor

This industry package includes everything you need to run your land surveying business efficiently with Odoo.

Basics

  • Use the Project app to manage your survey projects from initial consultation to final deliverables
  • Track leads and opportunities with the CRM app, ensuring no potential project slips through
  • Schedule site visits efficiently with the Appointment app
  • Create professional quotes and manage sales with the Sales app
  • Store and organize survey plans, property documents, and client files with the Documents app
  • Handle invoicing and payment tracking with the Invoicing app

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
