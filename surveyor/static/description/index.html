Surveying and Measurement App

The Odoo app for industrial companies involved in surveying and measurement activities provides a preconfigured database with all the necessary features to manage their operations. The app includes tools for property surveying, measurement of completed construction activities, and reporting. With this app, companies can efficiently manage their projects, track progress, and generate accurate reports. The pre-configured features make it easy to streamline operations and ensure precise measurements for all surveying and construction activities.
