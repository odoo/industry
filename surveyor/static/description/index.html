Odoo for Surveying & Mapping

This Odoo industry package provides a solid foundation for companies specializing in land surveying and measurement activities. It effectively manages the operational lifecycle of surveying projects, from initial site assessment to the delivery of final reports. The solution centralizes your project data, allowing you to track field activities, manage surveyor schedules, and store complex documents like blueprints and CAD files securely. By streamlining these administrative tasks, it ensures accurate billing and timely project completion.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Project App: Manage complex surveying sites as distinct projects, tracking tasks like initial measurements, data processing, and report generation.
  • Planning App: Optimize your field operations by visually scheduling surveyors and equipment to specific locations and times.
  • Documents App: Securely store, organize, and share heavy files such as topographic maps, CAD drawings, and compliance reports.
  • Knowledge App: Create a central repository for internal procedures, safety guidelines, and technical standards accessible to all staff.
  • CRM App: Track potential projects and manage relationships with developers, construction firms, and private property owners.
  • Sales App: Generate detailed proposals and contracts for surveying services, ensuring clear scope and pricing agreements.
  • Invoicing App: Bill clients accurately based on project milestones or time spent on site, maintaining healthy cash flow.
