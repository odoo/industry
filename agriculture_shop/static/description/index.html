Odoo for Agricultural Store

This Odoo industry module is tailored for agricultural retail businesses, supporting both B2B and B2C operations seamlessly. It handles the sales of diverse farming products, from seeds and pesticides to heavy equipment, ensuring precise inventory control. The solution integrates critical business functions like Point of Sale and Purchasing to run your store efficiently. With built-in eCommerce capabilities, you can easily expand your reach and modernize how you serve farmers and gardeners.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Point of Sale App: Manage in-store transactions efficiently, handling sales of seeds, tools, and supplies with ease.
  • Inventory App: Keep precise track of stock levels for diverse products like pesticides and equipment.
  • Sales App: Handle B2B orders and contracts for large farming clients, ensuring accurate pricing.
  • Purchase App: Streamline the procurement of farming products from suppliers, managing costs and lead times.
  • eCommerce App: Expand your reach by selling products online, offering convenience to modern farmers.
  • Website App: Build a professional online presence to showcase your catalog and attract new customers.
