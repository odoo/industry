Overview

About the industry

This Odoo industry module is tailored for agricultural retail businesses, supporting both B2B and B2C operations seamlessly. It handles the sales of diverse farming products, from seeds and pesticides to heavy equipment, ensuring precise inventory control. The solution integrates critical business functions like Point of Sale and Purchasing to run your store efficiently. With built-in eCommerce capabilities, you can easily expand your reach and modernize how you serve farmers and gardeners.

What is an Odoo Industry Package?

Odoo Industry packages are ready-to-go databases built by Odoo S.A. for specific business sectors. They come pre-configured with the right apps, settings, and data to save you serious setup time.

If standard Odoo features don't fully cover an industry's unique needs, extra information or capabilities can be added directly to the industry module using Odoo Studio or custom lines of code. (Please check our pricing section to see which plan applies to this module!)

What's Included?

  • Step-by-step guides inside the Knowledge app to walk you through the system and explain how to configure and use your database.

  • Pre-set configurations so you don't have to start from scratch (including the right apps and settings).

  • Optional demo data to help you visualize how your workflows will look.

💡 Pro Tip: Test the package with demo data on a separate test database first. Once you're comfortable with how it works, install a clean version without demo data on your live system.

Three Quick Things to Note:

  • 1

    Accounting is separate: It is never preinstalled out of the box, but you can easily add it whenever you're ready.

  • 2

    Inventory is kept basic: We purposely keep inventory settings simple at the start. It makes Odoo much easier to learn, and you can always turn on advanced tracking later.

  • 3

    Global scope: Industry modules are designed for worldwide use, meaning specific country-based localization needs are not covered out of the box.
