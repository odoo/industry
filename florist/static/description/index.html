Odoo for Florists

This setup is perfect for florists who offer fresh flowers, custom bouquets, and floral arrangements for weddings, birthdays, and corporate events.

Basics

  • Use the CRM app to manage customer relationships, flower inquiries, and event requests
  • Use the Sales app to handle orders for everyday bouquets and large-scale floral events
  • Use the Purchase app to source the freshest flowers from your vendors
  • Use the Point of Sale app to process in-store sales of flowers, bouquets, and gift items
  • Use the Website app to showcase your floral designs, services, and attract new customers

Included customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
