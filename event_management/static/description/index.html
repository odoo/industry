Odoo for Event Management

This industry package is ideal for businesses that organize events such as team-buildings, weddings, workshops, or corporate retreats. It covers the entire process from client inquiries to execution and invoicing, all managed through one integrated platform.

Basics

  • Capture client inquiries through the Website and send them to the CRM automatically.
  • Track and manage leads with the CRM and plan meetings or follow-ups.
  • Create professional service proposals and contracts using the Sales App.
  • Manage contacts including venues, suppliers, and partners with detailed profiles.
  • Turn confirmed bookings into structured project plans and timelines.
  • Manage equipment rentals and check availability in real-time.
  • Track stock like tents, signage, or furniture with the Inventory App.
  • Schedule equipment maintenance and repairs to ensure readiness.
  • Plan and assign staff to shifts using the Planning App.
  • Recruit event staff with job offers managed through the Recruitment App.
  • Keep your team in sync with a shared Calendar.
  • Create and send event invoices quickly and efficiently.
