  • From Prototype to Production: Move seamlessly from pre-project testing to full-scale manufacturing without re-entering data.

This module optimizes Odoo's core features to master the following specialized workflow

1. The Pre-Sales Phase

  • Lead Capture: Automate opportunity creation via integrated website forms or smart email aliases.

  • Pre-Project Scoping: Launch a dedicated "Pre-Project" directly from the opportunity to centralize technical requirements, feasibility studies, and initial prototyping.

  • Flexible Commercials: Manage multiple Sales Orders linked to a single project—seamlessly transitioning from a prototype contract to the final large-scale order.

2. The Production & Execution Hub

The Project App serves as your mission control, centralizing every moving part of the execution phase:

  • Unified Dashboard: Access Tasks, Timesheets, and Technical Documents in one click.

  • Integrated Supply Chain: Link Purchase Orders, Bills of Materials (BOM), and Manufacturing Orders (MO) directly to project.

  • Shop Floor Excellence: Utilize the Shop Floor App to provide operators with clear, interactive work instructions and real-time feedback loops.

  • Financial Intelligence: Leverage the project overview for real-time profitability tracking, comparing estimated vs. actual costs.

3. Delivery, Field Service & Maintenance

  • Deployment & Maintenance: Once delivered, schedule onsite installations or recurring maintenance via the Planning App.

  • Mobile Field Reports: Use customizable Worksheets to allow technicians to complete reports, capture photos, and collect client signatures directly from the field.

4. Advanced Ecosystem Integration

Extend your capabilities with Odoo's native power:

  • Quality Control: Trigger automated quality checks during inventory moves or critical manufacturing steps.

  • PLM (Product Lifecycle Management): Manage engineering change orders (ECOs) and maintain version control for complex BOMs.

  • Repair & Refurbishment: Track post-delivery adjustments and product repairs with full traceability.

  • Seamless Finance: Fully integrated Invoicing for milestones, progress billing, and vendor payments.
