Odoo for Yoga & Pilates Studio

This Odoo industry package is the perfect Zen master for your studio business. It centralizes everything from class scheduling and instructor management to membership subscriptions and billing. The solution empowers you to offer easy online booking for group classes or private sessions while automating recurring payments. By streamlining administrative tasks, it allows you to focus entirely on your students' wellbeing and the growth of your community.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Appointments App: Enable clients to easily book their favorite yoga or pilates classes online, reducing administrative friction.
  • Planning App: Efficiently assign instructors to classes based on their specific skills, availability, and expertise.
  • Subscription App: Secure revenue with automated recurring billing for memberships like "Unlimited Monthly Yoga."
  • Website App: Showcase your studio's philosophy and promote classes with a beautiful, integrated online presence.
  • Events App: Organize and manage special wellness workshops or retreats to deeply engage your community.
  • Survey App: Gather valuable feedback from students to ensure your classes and instructors meet their expectations.
  • Employees App: Track instructor schedules and manage their skills to ensure high-quality service delivery.
  • CRM App: Convert drop-in visitors into long-term members by tracking leads and nurturing relationships.
  • Sales App: Simplify the process of selling memberships, class packs, and merchandise with a unified system.
