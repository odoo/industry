Odoo for Yoga & Pilates Studio

This setup is perfect for yoga and pilates studios offering group classes, private sessions, memberships, and wellbeing events. Odoo helps you streamline your class scheduling, memberships, instructor management, and customer engagement in one place.

Basics

  • Use the Appointment App to schedule yoga and pilates classes for easy booking.
  • Use the Planning App to assign instructors based on expertise and availability.
  • Manage memberships like “The Flow Membership: Unlimited Yoga for one month” with the Subscription App.
  • Promote your studio, explain your approach, and enable online class bookings with the Website App.
  • Organize wellbeing sessions to engage your community using the Event App.
  • Collect feedback to ensure your wellbeing offerings align with your clients’ expectations using the Survey App.
  • Track schedules, roles, and expertise with the Employees App.
  • Convert leads into long-term members using the CRM App.
  • Simplify membership purchases and upgrades with the Sales App.

Included customizations

This industry relies only on standard Odoo features and does not include any customization.
