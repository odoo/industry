Odoo for Sports Club

This Odoo industry package is a game-changer for sports clubs that need to juggle court rentals, gear sales, and a busy cafeteria. It provides a unified system to manage your facilities, ensuring that every court booking is tracked and every coffee sold is accounted for. The solution simplifies the complex daily operations of a club, allowing you to focus on providing a great experience for your members rather than managing administrative chaos.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • Appointments App: Enable members to book courts or coaching sessions online 24/7, reducing front-desk workload and double-bookings.
  • Planning App: Optimize facility usage and staff schedules, ensuring the right resources are available for peak times and tournaments.
  • Point of Sale App: Streamline operations in your cafeteria and pro shop, handling sales of food, drinks, and sports gear with ease.
  • Inventory App: Keep track of rental equipment and retail stock, ensuring you always have the necessary gear and refreshments on hand.
  • Website & eCommerce App: Showcase your club's activities online and sell merchandise or memberships directly to your community.
  • CRM App: Manage member relationships and track potential leads for new sign-ups or corporate sponsorships.
  • Social Marketing App: Promote club events, tournaments, and special offers across social media to keep your community engaged.
