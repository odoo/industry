Sports Club Management App

The Odoo app for sports clubs provides a preconfigured database with all the necessary features to manage their activities. The app includes pre-configured products, court rental management, a website template, and tools to handle cafeteria operations and gear rentals. With this app, sports clubs can easily manage their bookings, track inventory, and handle invoicing. The website template allows clubs to showcase their facilities and services, while the pre-configured products make it easy to offer sports gear and refreshments to their members.
