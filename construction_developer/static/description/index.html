Odoo for Construction Developer

Master complex projects with a unified platform for estimation, planning, and billing. Odoo integrates subcontractor management and material forecasting into a single workflow. Gain real-time visibility into costs and schedules to ensure every phase stays on track and within budget.

Discover everything you need to know about this package by visiting the Knowledge App after installing it.

Basics

  • CRM App - Capture leads, schedule follow-ups, and track customer communications.
  • Sales App - Create detailed and structured quotations, send them via email or request online signature.
  • Project App - Manage construction sites as projects, organizing tasks into stages like "Foundation," "Framing," and "Finishing" with clear deadlines.
  • Timesheets App - Log work hours, track productivity, and measure project progress.
  • Field Service App - Plan on-site tasks with detailed worksheet when needed.
  • Purchase App - Automate the procurement of building materials, managing RFQs and purchase orders to ensure timely delivery to the site.
  • Inventory App - Monitor stock, manage materials, and track deliveries to sites.
  • Invoicing App - Generate invoices, manage payments, and reconcile accounts.
  • Planning App - Organize team shifts and resource allocation to ensure your projects are always running.
  • Documents App - Store contracts, designs, and project documentation in one central location.

Included customizations

  • Full width sale orders - Have all quotation details at your fingertips.
  • Line numbering - Iterate with your customers without any confusion about sale orders and invoices.
  • Progress management - Handle service delivery increments from projects for subsequent invoicing.
  • Remarks - Capture delivery remarks from project updates and calendar events, and follow them up from projects.

Pricing

This module involves multiple apps including Studio for various views, fields and relationships. It as well relies on lines of code through server actions and computed fields to bring extra capabilities, which leads to maintenance fees.
